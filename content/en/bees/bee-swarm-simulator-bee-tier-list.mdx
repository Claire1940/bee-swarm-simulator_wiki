<Section icon={Star} title="Event Bee Purchase and Gifting Priority">

Event bees are the backbone of any successful hive. Unlike standard bees, they do not require Royal Jellies to hatch; instead, you must purchase them using Tickets or earn them through specialized questlines. Because Tickets are a scarce resource in the early game, purchasing these bees in the correct order is vital to avoid stalling your progression.

Once hatched, your next major decision is choosing which bee to gift using Star Treats. Gifted event bees unlock unique, hive-wide passive bonuses that do not stack but provide massive, permanent multipliers to your stats.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Hive Builds: Mixed, Blue, Red, and White">

Choosing a hive direction is a major milestone. Many players make the mistake of choosing a specialized color too early. Switching to a single color early will make completing quests of opposing colors nearly impossible.

Once you are ready to transition, each color offers a completely distinct playstyle, resource cost, and operational demand.

<Section icon={Zap} title="Step-by-Step Hive Progression Guide">

To transition your hive efficiently without wasting massive amounts of honey or rare crafting materials, follow this structured upgrade path.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Establish the Starter Loop">
    Focus on unlocking your first 25 hive slots. Buy basic eggs, stick to starter fields, and focus entirely on completing Black Bear and Mother Bear quests. Do not roll for rare bees yet; quantity of bees is more important than quality at this stage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Assemble the Mountain Top Gear">
    Unlock the 30-bee gate and purchase the **Porcelain Dipper** and **Porcelain Port-O-Hive**. This gear setup will be your primary farming engine for a significant portion of your mid-game journey.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Unlock Mid-Game Crafted Equipment">
    Work toward crafting the **Honey Mask** in the Blend Shop. Acquire the Crimson and Cobalt Guards from the Red and Blue shops. Begin completing Science Bear quests to unlock Translators, which are required to communicate with key NPCs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Chasing the Spirit Petals">
    Complete Spirit Bear's demanding questlines. Use your first Spirit Petal to craft the **Petal Wand** for automatic pollen collection. Use your second petal to donate to the Wind Shrine to unlock **Windy Bee**, and your third for the **Petal Belt**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="The Supreme Star Amulet Grind">
    Discover 40 unique gifted bee types to unlock the purchase option for the Supreme Star Amulet. Once you have a solo Pop Star passive, roll your Royal Jellies to transition into a dedicated Blue Hive.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Royal Jelly Management">
When rolling for gifted bees to unlock your SSA, always use the "Auto-Jelly until Gifted" setting. Never manually click through thousands of jellies, as you risk rolling past rare mythic or gifted types by accident.
</Callout>
