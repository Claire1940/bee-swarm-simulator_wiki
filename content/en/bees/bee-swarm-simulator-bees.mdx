<Section icon={Map} title="Understanding Bee Swarm Simulator Bees Mechanics">

In the world of Roblox, **bee swarm simulator bees** are your primary tools for success. Every bee in your hive serves a specific purpose, whether it is collecting pollen from colorful fields, converting that pollen into honey at your hive, or defending you against aggressive bugs like Beetles and Spiders. As a player, your goal is to hatch eggs, discover rare species, and level up your swarm to unlock higher areas of the mountain.

The game features several rarities, ranging from Basic and Rare to Mythic and Event. While it is tempting to fill your hive with the shiniest bees immediately, understanding synergy is far more important. A well-balanced hive with gifted bonuses will always outperform a disorganized group of rare bees.

| Bee Rarity | Primary Source | Difficulty to Obtain | Main Utility |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Basic** | Basic Egg (Shop) | Very Easy | Early filler, Gifted bonus is 1.2x Pollen |
| **Rare** | Silver Egg / Royal Jelly | Easy | Entry-level tokens and color support |
| **Epic** | Gold Egg / Royal Jelly | Moderate | Essential mid-game buffs and abilities |
| **Legendary** | Diamond Egg / Royal Jelly | Hard | High-tier pollen collection and combat |

| **Event** | Tickets / Special Quests | Time-Consuming | Permanent unique abilities, no Royal Jelly |

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Hive Specialization: Choosing Your Color">

As you progress toward the late game, you will eventually need to specialize your hive color. However, choosing a color too early is a common mistake that can stall your quest progress. Most experts recommend staying as a "Mixed Hive" until you have completed all of Spirit Bear's quests and obtained the Supreme Star Amulet.

| Hive Color | Playstyle | Key Mythic Bee | Required Endgame Mask |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Blue Hive** | Relaxed, high capacity, macro-friendly | **Buoyant Bee** | Diamond Mask |
| **Red Hive** | Active, high damage, burst pollen | **Precise Bee** | Demon Mask |
| **White Hive** | Expensive, Goo-based, high investment | **Vector Bee** | Gummy Mask |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Mixed Phase">
    Focus on having one of every gifted bee type. This maximizes your hive bonuses and helps you complete quests from all Bears, which often require pollen from varied fields.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Unlocking the SSA">
    Collect 40 unique gifted bee types to unlock the Supreme Star Amulet. The "Passive" ability you roll on this amulet will dictate which color you should switch to.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Transitioning">
    Once you have a specific passive (like Pop Star for Blue), begin replacing your off-color bees with the appropriate Mythic bees for your chosen path.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Cost of Switching">
Switching hive colors costs trillions of honey in Royal Jelly and thousands of materials for gear. Never switch until you have at least 5-10 trillion honey saved up to ensure you can roll the necessary Mythic bees.
</Callout>

</Section>
