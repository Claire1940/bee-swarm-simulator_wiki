<Section icon={Map} title="How to Optimize Your Hive Layout">

The physical position of your bees matters. Because bees must travel from their slot to the field and back to the hive, placing your most active converters at the bottom of the hive reduces travel time, slightly increasing your Honey Per Hour (HPH).

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Place Tabby at the Bottom">
    Move your Tabby Bee to the center slot in the bottom row. This ensures it has the shortest flight path for converting its massive pollen loads.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Group Support Bees">
    Keep your Music and Commander bees near the middle. Their tokens are essential for maintaining 10x Focus and Melody stacks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Balance Gifted Types">
    Ensure you have one of every Gifted type (Rare through Mythic) before stacking duplicates. Duplicate gifted bonuses do not stack, but their individual stats are still useful.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Mutation Optimization">
    Use Neon Berries and Bitterberries to aim for **Gather Amount** or **Energy** mutations on your Mythic and Event bees.
  </Step>
</Steps>
