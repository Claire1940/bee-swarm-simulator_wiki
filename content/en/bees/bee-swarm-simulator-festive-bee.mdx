<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Abilities and Gifted Enhancements">

Understanding how to trigger and utilize the abilities of the Festive Bee is essential for maximizing your honey-per-second ratios. The bee specializes in field maintenance and cooperative buffs, generating tokens that assist both you and your teammates.

The first major ability is the **Red Bomb**, which collects red pollen from a cluster of surrounding flower patches. While this helps clear fields quickly, players must monitor their backpack capacity, as rapid bomb triggers can fill storage space faster than standard conversion rates can handle. The second ability is the **Honey Mark**. This creates a golden circle on the field for seven seconds. Standing inside this mark grants two conversion links and multiplies your conversion rate by 1.25, allowing you to empty your backpack without returning to the hive.

The signature move is the **Festive Gift**. When activated, the bee drops a gift box that yields rewards for every player on the server. The quality of these rewards scales based on whether the bee is gifted and if seasonal events are active.

| Ability | Trigger | Primary Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Red Bomb | Random | Gathers red pollen from 13 nearby patches |
| Honey Mark | Random | Spawns conversion zone with x1.25 rate |
| Festive Gift | Random | Drops server-wide tokens containing resources |

Feeding a Star Treat to the Festive Bee transforms it into its gifted state. This unlock is highly beneficial for red-heavy builds, providing a permanent +15% Red Pollen multiplier to your entire hive. Additionally, gifted status upgrades the item pool of the Festive Gift, increasing the chance of receiving rare items like gumdrops, treats, and crafting materials.
