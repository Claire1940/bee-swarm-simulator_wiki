<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Understanding Gifted Bees and Hive Bonuses">

Building the perfect hive requires a deep understanding of the **bee swarm simulator gifted bee tier list** and how gifted bonuses stack. Gifted bees are specialized variants of normal bees that offer a 1.5x multiplier to their base stats, collect pollen faster, and unlock a unique permanent passive called a Hive Bonus. Unlike standard stats, these Hive Bonuses apply to your entire character, making them the single most important factor when optimizing your honey production.

While a normal bee only contributes to local field collection, a gifted bee acts as a passive multiplier for your entire account. However, players must understand that duplicate gifted hive bonuses do not stack. Having two Gifted Commander Bees will not grant you a double Critical Chance bonus. Because of this limitation, a well-optimized mid-game hive should aim to have exactly one of each gifted bee type to maximize active passive coverage.
