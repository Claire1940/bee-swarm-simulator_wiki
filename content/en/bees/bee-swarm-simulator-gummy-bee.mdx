<Section icon={Map} title="Fast Gumdrop Farming Methods">

Amassing 2,500 Gumdrops can feel like a monumental task, especially for early to mid-game players. However, utilizing structured farming routines dramatically cuts down the time required. The most efficient approach combines active boss battles, smart planter placement, and targeted ticket exchanges.

For active players, the Stump Snail is a goldmine. While defeating it takes significant time due to its 30-million health pool, the rewards are unmatched. It drops between 50 and 2,500 Gumdrops, alongside 50 guaranteed tickets. You can immediately take those tickets to the Gumdrop Dispenser near the 20-Bee Gate to purchase an additional 150 Gumdrops.

| Method | Gumdrop Yield | Time Investment | Difficulty Level |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Gumdrop Dispenser** | 3 per Ticket | Instant | Very Low |
| **Stump Snail** | 50 to 2,500 | High (AFK) | Medium |
| **Candy Planter** | Variable | Medium | Low |
| **Gummy Sprout** | 30 to 50 | Low | Medium |
| **Puffshrooms** | Variable | Medium | High |
| **Stick Bug Challenge** | Variable | Low | High |

Using planters is another excellent passive strategy. The Candy Planter is cheap to craft and highly effective. When grown in the Stump Field, it yields a higher concentration of Gumdrops compared to other fields. Additionally, keep an eye on the Sprout Spawner. There is a small chance to summon a Gummy Sprout, which yields between 30 and 50 Gumdrops when popped.

For high-level players, participating in the Stick Bug Challenge and chasing Puffshrooms every 30 minutes will yield a steady stream of crafting materials. If you prefer an automated approach, configuring a safe macro to run bug runs or farm in the Pine Tree Forest will steadily build your inventory overnight.

<Callout type="tip" title="Planter Optimization">

</Callout>
