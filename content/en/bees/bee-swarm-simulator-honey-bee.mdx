<Section icon={Trophy} title="Honey Bee's Honey Hunt: Repeatable Quests">

The primary interaction with Honey Bee outside of special events is the "Honey Bee's Honey Hunt." This is a repeatable quest that scales in difficulty based on how many times you have completed it. The core objective is always the same: collect Honey Tokens. These tokens are the small yellow coins that drop from various sources throughout the map.

The number of tokens required is calculated as `500 * n`, where `n` is your current quest number. While the task is simple, the rewards are highly beneficial for mid-game players looking to boost their honey production.

| Quest Number | Token Requirement | Primary Reward | Extra Reward Chance |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Quest 1** | 500 Tokens | 11,000 Honey + Boost | 24% Ticket / 24% Treats |

| **Quest 50** | 25,000 Tokens | 2.51M Honey + Boost | 24% Ticket / 24% Treats |

<Section icon={Target} title="Honey Bee 2026 Quest Walkthrough">

The Honey Bee 2026 quest, often associated with the Beesmas event, is notoriously difficult and requires significant preparation. To even receive this quest, you must be wearing the Honey Mask and have finished Black Bear's Honey Wreath quest. The requirements focus on high-volume token collection and specific field mechanics.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Bloom Petal Collection">
    Collect 10 million Honey from Bloom Petals. The best strategy here is to choose a field that matches your Hive Color (Red, Blue, or White) and use an Oil to increase move speed while grinding the petals.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Honey Conversion Links">
    Complete 100,000 Honey Conversion Links. This requires bees that drop Honey Mark tokens, such as Honey Bee, Carpenter Bee, or Festive Bee. Standing in a Honey Mark grants 2 Conversion Links per second, stacking up to 3 times.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Honey Token Mass Collection">
    Collect 100,000 Honey Tokens. Speed this up by defeating the Mondo Chick, activating Honey Storms, and popping sprouts. Catching Coconuts from a Coconut Bee is also a very reliable source.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Honey Storm Mastery">
    Collect 10,000 tokens from Honey Storms. This is the hardest part for solo players. Join public servers or coordinate with friends to chain multiple Honey Storms. Position yourself between the Clover Field and Wealth Clock for optimal coverage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Hub and Sunflower Fields">
    Collect 1,000 Honey Tokens in the Hub Field and 1,000 in the Sunflower Field. Use Coconuts and Sprouts in these specific locations. Since these are lower-level fields, tokens spawn more frequently than in the Mountain Top.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Honey Mark Requirements">
If you do not have at least 3-4 bees capable of producing Honey Marks (like Carpenter Bees), the Conversion Link task will take exponentially longer. Consider temporarily adjusting your hive composition for this quest.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Optimal Honey Token Farming Strategies">

To complete Honey Bee's demands efficiently, you need to utilize every mechanic that spawns yellow tokens. Not all methods are equal; some provide a massive burst of tokens, while others are steady but slow.

<CardGrid cols={3}>
  <Card title="Mob Hunting">
    - **Mondo Chick**: Drops massive clusters of Honey Tokens upon defeat.
    - **Stick Bug**: Spawns Stick Nymphs that drop tokens continuously.
    - **Windy Bee**: Tornadoes collect and redistribute tokens for easy pickup.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Field Mechanics">
    - **Coconuts**: Dropping and catching Coconuts is the fastest way to spawn 10 tokens every 10 seconds.
    - **Coin Scatter**: The Honey Mask ability "Coin Scatter" converts honey directly into tokens on the field.
    - **Sprouts**: Rare sprouts provide higher token density.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Social Buffs">
    - **Honey Storm**: Summons a map-wide rain of tokens.
    - **Meteor Shower**: Provides high-value tokens and rare items.
    - **Party Sprouts**: Best for group farming sessions.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>
