To systematically target this bee, follow these steps to optimize your resources and avoid wasting valuable honey during your rolls.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Gather Royal Jellies">
    Acquire a substantial stack of Royal Jellies. You can purchase them from the Royal Jelly Dispenser or farm them by defeating bosses like the King Beetle, Tunnel Bear, and Coconut Crab.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Configure Auto-Roll Settings">
    Go to your hive, select the slot containing a bee you wish to replace, and open the Auto-Roll menu. Set the system to stop on "Legendary" to ensure you do not accidentally roll past a Lion Bee.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Initiate the Roll Sequence">
    Begin rolling your Jellies. On average, it takes about 80 to 100 Royal Jellies to hit a Legendary bee. Since there are 8 Legendary bees, you have a 12.5% chance of getting a Lion Bee once a Legendary is triggered.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Manage Your Honey Reserves">
