<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Pulsar vs. Other Pro Shop Gear: A Comparative Analysis">

When evaluating the **bee swarm simulator pulsar**, players often compare it to other tools available in the Pro Shop, such as the Super-Scooper and the Electro-Magnet. The Pro Shop serves as a major transition point for early-game players, and choosing the wrong tool can delay your progression to the Mountain Top Shop.

The biggest drawback of the Pulsar is its tool speed. It is tied with the Gummyballer and the Sticker-Seeker as one of the slowest tools in the game, featuring a full 1-second cooldown between swings. In contrast, tools like the Super-Scooper and Electro-Magnet swing every 0.5 seconds, providing a much smoother and more active playstyle.

Saving your honey to jump straight from the Super-Scooper to the Electro-Magnet or Scissors is generally considered the most efficient path.

<Section icon={Target} title="How to Farm the Bee Swarm Simulator Pulsar Sticker">

While the Pulsar tool may be subpar for gathering pollen, it has recently become incredibly important due to the introduction of the sticker system. The **Pulsar Sticker** is a rare tool sticker that can only be obtained by actively using the Pulsar tool. 

When farming with the Pulsar, there is a very small, random chance that your tool will "dig up" a sticker. When this happens, a rainbow notification will appear in your chat log, and a physical sticker token will spawn on the field. You must walk over this token to claim your sticker; if you are macroing and not paying attention, there is a moderate risk that the token will despawn before you collect it.

To maximize your chances of finding this elusive collectible, you must target specific fields that feature boosted drop rates. 

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Equip the Pulsar Tool">
    Make sure the Pulsar tool is actively equipped in your hand. You cannot get the sticker using any other tool.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Travel to the Spider Field">
    The **Spider Field** is the designated field where the Pulsar has a significantly higher chance of digging up its corresponding sticker.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Alternative: Visit the Hive Hub">
    If the Spider Field is crowded, head to the **Hive Hub**. All tool sticker drop rates are boosted to their maximum values inside the Hive Hub public area.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Collect the Token">
    Listen for the distinct chime and watch for the rainbow text notification. Immediately run to the spinning token to add the Pulsar Sticker to your Sticker Book.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Hive Hub Boosting">
Farming in the Hive Hub is highly recommended if you want to socialize or trade while grinding. The drop rate boost in the Hive Hub is identical to the boost found in the Spider Field.
</Callout>

</Section>
