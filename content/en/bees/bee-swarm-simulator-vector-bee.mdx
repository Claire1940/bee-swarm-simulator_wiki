<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Core Abilities and Stats">

The Vector Bee's efficiency is determined by its ability to generate and sustain Marks. These Marks provide localized buffs that, when stacked, transform a standard field into a high-yield farming zone. Below is a detailed breakdown of the Vector Bee's base statistics and its primary active abilities.

| Statistic | Base Value (Level 1) | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Pollen Collection** | 20 | Collected every 4 seconds |
| **Honey Conversion** | 800 | Converted every 4 seconds |
| **Attack Power** | 4 | Moderate damage for a Mythic bee |
| **Speed** | 24 | High mobility for ability placement |
| **Energy** | 40 | Standard Mythic energy pool |

Beyond raw stats, the Vector Bee's value lies in its two main tokens: **Pollen Mark** and **Triangulate**.

### Pollen Mark
This ability marks a large random area on the field for 8 seconds. While standing inside the Mark, all pollen collection is increased by 50%. This effect can stack up to three times, leading to a massive 150% boost in collection efficiency. For players utilizing the **bee swarm simulator vector bee**, maintaining these stacks is the difference between a mediocre run and a record-breaking farm.

### Triangulate
Triangulate draws a triangle between the player and the bee. This geometric field collects pollen from every flower it touches. The amount collected is significantly higher than standard gathering and scales with the bee's level and your hive's white pollen multipliers.

<Callout type="warning" title="Strategy Alert">
Triangulate is highly dependent on player positioning. If you stand still, the triangles will be small and inefficient. Always keep moving to maximize the area of effect.
</Callout>
