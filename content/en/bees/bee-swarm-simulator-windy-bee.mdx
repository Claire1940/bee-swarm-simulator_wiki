<Section icon={Target} title="Unlocking Windy Bee: The Wind Shrine Path">

Unlocking the **bee swarm simulator windy bee** is one of the most significant milestones in a player's progression. It requires access to the 35-Bee Zone and the completion of several high-level tasks. You cannot simply buy this bee with tickets; you must earn its favor through the Wind Shrine.

The process is divided into two distinct phases: unlocking the ability to receive the bee and then successfully "taming" it through donations.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Obtain a Spirit Petal">
    You must complete the 10th, 20th, or 30th quest from **Spirit Bear**. Most players recommend using the second Spirit Petal for Windy Bee, as the first is usually better spent on the **Petal Wand** to improve general farming.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Donate the Petal to the Wind Shrine">
    Travel to the Wind Shrine located on the mountain top. Interact with it and donate your Spirit Petal. This does not give you the bee immediately; it only unlocks the *possibility* of receiving it from future donations.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Farm Cloud Vials">
    Defeat the **Wild Windy Bee** that occasionally spawns in various fields. Collecting the tokens it drops will reward you with **Cloud Vials**. You will need anywhere from 100 to 500+ vials depending on your "Favor" level.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Spam Donations and Pray">
    Donate Cloud Vials in stacks (usually 10 at a time) to the Shrine. Every donation increases your invisible **Favor** stat. Eventually, the Shrine will respond with a unique message, and the Windy Bee Egg will be added to your inventory.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Donation Strategy">
Do not donate your Spirit Petal until you are ready to commit to the grind. Once donated, it cannot be reclaimed. Ensure you have a steady supply of Cloud Vials by checking the wind chimes frequently for Wild Windy Bee spawns.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Advanced Tactics: Mastering the Tornado">

The Tornado ability of the **bee swarm simulator windy bee** is its most complex mechanic. Unlike other bees that collect from a single spot, the Tornado is an area-of-effect (AoE) tool. Its pathing is semi-random but tends to gravitate toward areas with higher flower density or where the player is currently standing.

**Maximizing Tornado Yield:**
1. **Field Selection**: Use Windy Bee in fields with high-tier flowers (like the Pepper Patch or Pine Tree Forest). The Tornado's collection is based on a percentage of the flower's value, so better flowers equal more honey.
2. **Link with Sprinklers**: Ensure your Supreme Star Sprinkler is active. Tornadoes collect more pollen from "upgraded" flowers that have been watered.
3. **Ability Stacking**: Wait for **Baby Love** (from Baby Bee) or **Melody** (from Music Bee) to proc before collecting Tornado tokens. This multiplies the pollen gathered by the vortex.
