<Section icon={Map} title="Beginner Starter Path and Core Gameplay Loop">

The core gameplay loop centers on gathering pollen, returning to your hive to convert it into honey, and spending that honey to expand your swarm and upgrade your tools. Navigating this cycle efficiently prevents early bottlenecks and ensures a steady supply of resources.

<Callout type="tip" title="Farming Efficiency">
Always collect ability tokens dropped by your bees while farming in fields. These tokens trigger critical buffs, such as focus, haste, and critical power, which drastically increase your pollen collection rate.
</Callout>

  <Step num="3" title="Convert Pollen to Honey at Your Hive">

  <Step num="4" title="Purchase Eggs and Expand Your Swarm">

  <Step num="5" title="Prioritize Early Bear Quests">

### Key Gear Milestones Explained

As you scale your operations, certain gear purchases will redefine your farming speed:

- **Porcelain Dipper**: This is your most important mid-game tool. It collects massive amounts of pollen and converts a portion of it instantly, drastically reducing your trips back to the hive.
- **Honey Mask**: Crafted in the Mountain Top Shop, this mask grants the "Coin Shower" passive ability, which converts pollen into honey directly in the field.
- **Petal Wand**: Obtained by trading a Spirit Petal at the Wind Shrine, this tool automatically converts pollen and fires a projectile that harvests flowers in a straight line.
