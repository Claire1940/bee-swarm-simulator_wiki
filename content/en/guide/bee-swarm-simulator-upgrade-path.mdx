<Section icon={Shield} title="Optimal Gear Purchase Order">

The gear you equip determines how fast you collect pollen and convert it into honey. Buying gear out of order can leave you with a massive backpack that takes too long to fill, or a powerful tool that overflows a tiny container instantly. 

During the starter and early stages, focus on items from the Noob Shop and the Pro Shop. Avoid buying every single accessory; instead, skip minor upgrades to save honey for high-impact tools like the Golden Rake.

| Item | Shop Location | Cost | Upgrade Priority |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Magnet | Noob Shop | 14,000 Honey | High (replaces basic scoop) |
| Vacuum | Noob Shop | 12,000 Honey | Medium (good alternative collector) |
| Poinsettia | Noob Shop | 28,000 Honey | High (best early starter bag) |
| Clipper | Pro Shop | 85,000 Honey | High (essential for pollen conversion) |
| Golden Rake | Pro Shop | 800,000 Honey | Very High (main tool until Mountain Top) |

Once you reach the Mountain Top Shop, the upgrade cost increases significantly. The Porcelain Dipper is the most important mid-game tool because its ability collects pollen from multiple flowers simultaneously, boosting your farming speed exponentially.

| Item | Shop Location | Key Materials | Progression Value |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Porcelain Dipper | Mountain Top | 150M Honey | Mandatory (best mid-game tool) |
| Porcelain Port-O-Hive | Mountain Top | 250M Honey | Mandatory (essential backpack capacity) |
| Honey Mask | Blend Shop | 5 Gold Eggs, 50 Enzymes | Extremely High (coin scatter passive) |
| Crimson Guard | Red Shop | 100 Red Extract, 50 Glitter | High (essential red field defense/pollen) |
| Cobalt Guard | Blue Shop | 100 Blue Extract, 50 Glitter | High (essential blue field defense/pollen) |

<Callout type="warning" title="Material Preservation Warning">
Never spend rare crafting ingredients like Enzymes, Glitter, or Extracts on random Royal Jelly rolls. Save every single ingredient for crafting the Honey Mask and the Crimson/Cobalt Guards.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Mid-Game Transition Guide">

Transitioning from early-game to mid-game is where many players get stuck. This phase requires grinding millions of honey while completing complex quests from Science Bear and Black Bear. Follow these steps to navigate this critical transition smoothly.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Secure the Porcelain Dipper">
    Save 150 million honey and buy the Porcelain Dipper from the Mountain Top Shop. Do not buy the Porcelain Port-O-Hive first, as the Dipper increases your collection rate, making it easier to grind for the backpack later.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Reach 30 Bees & Unlock the Merchant">
    Expand your hive to 30 slots. This unlocks access to the 30-bee gate, allowing you to interact with the Wind Shrine, purchase items from the specialty shops, and start preparing for Spirit Bear quests.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Craft the Honey Mask">
    Gather 5 Gold Eggs, 50 Enzymes, 250 Treat, and 500 Sunflower Seeds. Craft the Honey Mask at the Blender. Its Coin Scatter passive converts a portion of your full bag into honey tokens instantly on the field, saving you countless trips back to the hive.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Acquire the Crimson and Cobalt Guards">
    Collect 100 Red Extracts, 100 Blue Extracts, and 50 Glitter. Purchase the Crimson Guard and Cobalt Guard from their respective color shops. These guards provide massive defensive bonuses and boost red and blue pollen collection.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Gold Egg Tip">
Do not use the Gold Eggs you receive from quests. Save them specifically for crafting the Honey Mask, or you will be forced to buy them with valuable Tickets later.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Hive Color Specialization & Builds">

A common mistake in the **bee swarm simulator upgrade path** is choosing a hive color too early. Players often see endgame players with all-blue or all-red hives and try to replicate them before they are ready. 

You must remain a **Mixed Hive** until you unlock the Supreme Star Amulet (SSA), which requires discovering 40 unique gifted bee types. Choosing a color early makes completing quests from Spirit Bear and Science Bear nearly impossible, as they require collecting billions of pollen from fields of all colors.

| Build Type | Main Playstyle | Gear Requirement | Best Fields |

<Callout type="success" title="Recommended First Color">
