<Section icon={Map} title="Understanding the Wind Shrine Mechanics">

The **bee swarm simulator wind shrine** is a pivotal structure for players who have reached the mid-to-late game stages of their progression. Located behind the 35-bee gate, perched on a cliff near the Spirit Bear, this shrine allows players to "sacrifice" or donate various items in exchange for favors, field boosts, and rare item drops. Unlike standard shops or quest givers, the Wind Shrine operates on a system of probability and hidden "favor" points, making it one of the most complex systems in the game to master.

In 2026, the meta for the Wind Shrine focuses on maximizing "Favor," a hidden stat that scales with the value of your donations. The more you donate, the more the Wind Shrine "likes" you, which increases the quality of your rewards and the likelihood of successfully taming a Windy Bee. Players often start by donating items they have in excess, but strategic players save high-value materials for specific boost windows to maximize their honey-per-hour rates.

| Item Category | Common Examples | Primary Purpose at Shrine |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Basic Materials** | Sunflower Seeds, Blueberries | Minor favor gains / Small boosts |
| **Crafted Items** | Enzymes, Oil, Glue | Moderate favor / Targeted field boosts |
| **High-Value Items** | Star Jelly, Gold Eggs | Significant favor / Massive honey rewards |
| **Quest Items** | Spirit Petals | Unlocking the Windy Bee encounter |
| **Consumables** | Cloud Vials | Increasing favor / Taming the Windy Bee |

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="The Hidden Favor System and Taming Windy Bee">

The most sought-after reward from the **bee swarm simulator wind shrine** is the Windy Bee. However, obtaining this Event Bee is not as simple as buying an egg. It requires a long-term commitment to the Favor system. Every time you donate an item, your hidden Favor score increases. This score never resets, meaning every small donation contributes to your eventual success.

The process begins by donating a Spirit Petal. Once a petal has been donated, the shrine has a chance to release a Windy Bee Beequip or a Tamed Windy Bee egg when you subsequently donate Cloud Vials. Without the initial Spirit Petal donation, you can still get boosts and items, but the bee itself will remain out of reach.

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step: Unlocking the Windy Bee">

Unlocking the Windy Bee is a rite of passage for late-game players. Follow these steps to ensure you don't waste your resources at the **bee swarm simulator wind shrine**.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Reach the 35-Bee Zone">
    Farm enough honey to purchase 35 hive slots. This unlocks the mountain top area where the Spirit Bear and the Wind Shrine reside.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Complete Spirit Bear Quests">
    Complete the first 10 quests from Spirit Bear to receive your first Spirit Petal. Complete the next 10 for your second petal.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Donate the Spirit Petal">
    Interact with the Wind Shrine and select the Spirit Petal from your inventory. The shrine will consume it, and the wind will "shift," acknowledging your sacrifice.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Build Favor with Cloud Vials">
    Defeat Wild Windy Bees (found by looking for clouds in fields) to collect Cloud Vials. Donate these vials in stacks of 5 or 10 to the shrine.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="The Taming Moment">
    Continue donating Cloud Vials. Eventually, the shrine will respond with a unique message, and the Windy Bee will join your hive.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Pro Strategy">
Donating 5-10 Cloud Vials at a time is the most efficient way to check for the Windy Bee unlock while still building favor. Donating 1 vial at a time is statistically less efficient for triggering the reward.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Donation Rewards and Field Boosts">

Beyond the Windy Bee, the **bee swarm simulator wind shrine** is a powerful tool for triggering "Winds." Winds are field-specific multipliers that can increase your pollen gain by up to 10x or more. This is essential for completing high-level quests that require billions of pollen from a single field.

The type of item you donate influences which field receives the boost. While there is an element of randomness, certain patterns have been identified by the community.

| Donated Item | Likely Field Boost | Secondary Reward |

| **Strawberry** | Strawberry Field / Mushroom / Rose | Red Extracts |

</Callout>
