<Section icon={Swords} title="Essential Bee Composition for Blue Hives">

Building a **bee swarm simulator blue hive** is an exercise in mythic bee management. Your hive will eventually consist of nearly 50% mythic bees. The synergy between Tadpole Bees (which generate bubbles and frogs) and Buoyant Bees (which provide capacity and golden balloons) is what generates the massive honey numbers seen in top-tier leaderboards.

While mythics are the stars, your support bees are equally important. Gifted bonuses from rare and epic bees provide the critical multipliers needed to scale your pollen collection.

**Core Blue Hive Bee Roles:**

- **Tadpole Bee**: Generates bubbles that collect pollen and summon frogs. Frogs are essential for picking up tokens and boosting bubble growth.
- **Buoyant Bee**: The "carry" of the hive. It provides massive capacity through balloons and boosts your pollen multipliers significantly.
- **Support Bees**: Music Bees for Melody/Focus, and Gifted Commander/Looker bees for critical power.

<Callout type="warning" title="Gifted Basic Bee">
Even in a pure blue hive, a **Gifted Basic Bee** is mandatory. Its 1.2x Total Pollen multiplier is a separate calculation that boosts your final honey output more than almost any blue-specific bee.
</Callout>

| Bee Type | Recommended Count | Primary Role |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Buoyant Bee** | 18 - 22 | Capacity, Balloons, Pollen Boost |
| **Tadpole Bee** | 8 - 12 | Bubbles, Frogs, Blue Bomb tokens |
| **Music Bee** | 3 - 4 | Melody (Crit Power) and Focus |
| **Fuzzy Bee** | 1 (Gifted) | Pollination and flower upgrades |
| **Digital Bee** | 1 | Late-game token duplication |

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Step-by-Step: Transitioning to a Blue Hive">

Moving from a mid-game mixed hive to a specialized **bee swarm simulator blue hive** requires careful resource management. If you spend all your Royal Jelly too early, you might end up with a "broken" hive that can't complete quests for other colors.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Unlock the Diamond Mask">
    Save your Diamond Eggs! You need 5 Diamond Eggs to craft the mask. Never use them for hatching unless you already have the mask.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Secure the SSA">
    Farm 40 unique Gifted Bees. Use Star Jellies and Sunflower Seeds/Blueberries to gift your lower-tier bees first.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Roll for Pop Star">
    Expect to spend 50 billion to 500 billion honey rolling for a decent Pop Star amulet. Ideally, look for Blue Pollen and Critical Power stats.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Mythic Jelly Farming">
    Use the "Auto-Jelly until Mythic" setting. You will need roughly 20,000 to 50,000 Royal Jelly per Mythic bee on average.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Leveling the Hive">
    Blue hives rely on bee levels to stay in the field longer. Aim for a full Level 15 hive before pushing for the Tide Popper.
  </Step>
</Steps>
