<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Early Blue Hive Build and Bee Composition">

The core mechanic of a **bee swarm simulator early blue hive** revolves around two mythic bees: Buoyant Bee and Tadpole Bee. Buoyant Bees inflate balloons that store massive amounts of pollen, which you then convert at your hive for multiplied honey gains. Tadpole Bees spawn frogs that generate bubbles, which collect pollen, grow blue flowers, and charge your Pop Star passive.

In the early stages of this build, you do not need all of your mythic bees to be gifted. Instead, focus on reaching a functional ratio of Buoyant and Tadpole bees while filling the rest of your hive with essential blue-support and utility bees.

Maintaining this multiplier requires consistent active play or structured farming paths.

| Field Name | Blue Flower Ratio | Best Use Case | Risk Factor |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |

| **Bamboo Field** | 70% Blue | Quest completion, secondary boosting | Medium (Spiders can disrupt farming) |
| **Stump Field** | 100% Blue | Long-term AFK farming, Snail clearing | High (Stump Snail blocks center unless defeated) |

To optimize your farming runs, always prioritize the Pine Tree Forest. It is a completely blue field, meaning your blue pollen multipliers will apply to every single flower you clear.
