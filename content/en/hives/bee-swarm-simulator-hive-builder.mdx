<Section icon={Map} title="Why You Need a Bee Swarm Simulator Hive Builder Strategy">

Building an optimized hive in Roblox's Bee Swarm Simulator is not about randomly placing legendary insects. To maximize pollen collection and honey conversion, players must follow a strict progression path. A dedicated **bee swarm simulator hive builder** strategy helps you understand when to keep a balanced setup and when to transition into specialized color builds.

During the early and mid-game stages, staying mixed is highly recommended. Attempting to force a single color too early slows down quest completion, limits your resource gathering across different fields, and drains your hard-earned honey. Specialized builds require specific endgame gear, passive amulets, and mythic bee compositions to function correctly.

| Hive Type | Progression Stage | Active Difficulty | Primary Focus |
|--- |--- |--- |--- |
| **Mixed Hive** | Early to Mid Game | Low | Quest completion, badge grinding, general farming |
| **Blue Hive** | Late to Endgame | Medium | High capacity, balloon storage, steady solo gains |
| **Red Hive** | Late to Endgame | High | High-burst active boosts, flame precision, combat |
| **White Hive** | Expensive Endgame | Very High | Massive investment, goo-heavy fields, mark synergy |

<Callout type="warning" title="Progression Warning">
Switching your hive color before securing the Supreme Star Amulet (SSA) is a common mistake. Without the correct passive abilities, specialized color builds perform significantly worse than a standard mixed hive.

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Hive Progression Guide">

Transitioning your hive layout must be done in stages. Rushing the process will ruin your farming efficiency. Follow this structured roadmap to build your ideal setup without wasting precious resources.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Maintain a Mixed Hive">
    Focus on hatching a diverse group of bees to complete Black Bear, Mother Bear, and Science Bear quests. Keep a balanced mix of red, blue, and white pollen gatherers. Do not waste Royal Jelly trying to roll specific mythics yet.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Acquire Mountain Top Gear">
    Purchase the Porcelain Dipper and Porcelain Port-O-Hive from the Mountain Top Shop. This gear setup is your primary farming engine throughout the entire mid-game phase.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Unlock Mid-Game Crafted Masks">
    Craft the Honey Mask, Bubble Mask, or Fire Mask depending on your immediate progress. The Honey Mask is highly recommended for its coin scatter ability, which significantly boosts solo conversion rates.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Unlock the Supreme Star Amulet">
    Grind gifted bee types until you discover 40 unique gifted bees. This unlocks the ability to purchase the Supreme Star Amulet, which is the absolute foundation of any specialized hive builder plan.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Roll for Specialized Passives">
    Spend your honey rolling for the specific passive ability that matches your chosen color (such as Pop Star for blue). Once secured, gradually replace your filler bees with corresponding mythic and support bees.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Essential Bee Rankings for Hive Builders">

Not all bees are created equal. Some provide critical hive-wide bonuses that must be maintained regardless of your final color choice, while others are highly specialized.

### Event Bee Purchase Priority

Event bees are purchased using tickets or unlocked through specific quest lines. They do not have a gifted requirement to be useful, but gifting them should be your top priority.

| Event Bee | Priority Level | Main Utility | Gifted Hive Bonus |
|--- |--- |--- |--- |
| **Tabby Bee** | Top Priority | Scaling pollen gather, critical strike power | +50% Critical Power |
| **Photon Bee** | High Priority | Instant pollen collection, beam storm | +5% Instant Conversion |
| **Vicious Bee** | High Priority | Massive combat damage, monster respawn reduction | -15% Monster Respawn Time |
| **Gummy Bee** | High Priority | Goo generation, honey mark support | +5% Honey Per Pollen |
| **Puppy Bee** | Low Priority | Treat discount, fetch toy support | +20% Bond from Treats |

### Mythic Bee Specialization

Mythic bees are the backbone of late-game builds. You will need to stack multiple copies of these bees to optimize your collection rates.

| Mythic Bee | Primary Hive Alignment | Core Ability |
|--- |--- |--- |
| **Tadpole Bee** | Blue | Summons frogs, spawns bubbles, hydrates flowers |
| **Buoyant Bee** | Blue / Mixed | Spawns golden balloons, increases capacity, converts pollen |
| **Spicy Bee** | Red | Summons fire bees, spawns rage tokens, creates flames |
| **Precise Bee** | Red | Shoots targets for instant conversion, increases super-crit chance |
| **Vector Bee** | White | Creates triangulate marks, collects massive pollen along lines |
| **Fuzzy Bee** | Support (All Colors) | Pollinates flowers, upgrades flower tiers for better yields |

<Callout type="success" title="Gifted Priority">
Always use your Star Treats on Event Bees first, starting with Tabby Bee. Unlike regular bees, Event Bees cannot be turned gifted using favorite treats, making Star Treats the only reliable method.
</Callout>
