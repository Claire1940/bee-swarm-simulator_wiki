Navigating the journey from a single bee to a full 50-slot hive requires a balanced approach. Following this path ensures you aren't "hive-rich but gear-poor," a common mistake where players have many **bee swarm simulator hive slots** but lack the tools to fill their bags efficiently.

  <Step num="1" title="The Starter Loop">
    Focus on the first 10 slots. Use the basic honey you collect from the Sunflower and Dandelion fields. Don't worry about bee rarity yet; just fill the slots with whatever eggs you find or buy.

  <Step num="2" title="Reaching the 25-Bee Gate">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Mid-Game Grind (35 Slots)">

  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Late Game Optimization (40-45 Slots)">
    Now you must decide on a hive color (Red, Blue, or White). Your slot purchases will slow down here. Focus on leveling your bees to level 12+ and obtaining the Snail or Coconut amulets.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="The Final Push to 50">
    The last 5 slots are purely for endgame players. These slots are usually filled with Mythic bees (like Buoyant or Precise bees) to maximize the efficiency of specialized hive builds.
