<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Guide to Boosting as a White Hive">

To make significant progress as a white hive, you cannot rely on passive, AFK grinding. Instead, you must perform coordinated "boosts." A boost involves stacking multiple temporary multipliers—such as field boosts, winds, nectars, and consumables—to generate hundreds of billions, or even trillions, of honey in a single 15-to-30-minute window.

Because white hives rely heavily on active items like Gumdrops and Field Dice, preparation is key. Follow this step-by-step procedure to execute a highly profitable white hive boost.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Prepare Your Nectars and Buffs">
    Before starting, ensure your **Satisfying Nectar** and **Motivating Nectar** are stacked to maximum duration. These nectars provide massive boosts to white pollen, coin conversion, and bee ability rates. Activate your **Wealth Clock** to secure a high honey-per-pollen multiplier.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Donate to the Wind Shrine">
    Donate a valuable item (such as a **Gold Egg**, **Silver Egg**, or **Field Dice**) to the Wind Shrine to secure high-tier **White Field Winds**. Aim for winds on **Spider Field**, **Coconut Field**, or **Pineapple Patch**, as these are the premier white fields on the mountain.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Roll Field Boosts">
    Use your **Red/Blue/White Field Boosters** or spend **Glitter** directly on your target field to secure a **3x Field Boost**. If you do not get your desired white field, use Field Dice or Smoothies to reroll until you land a high multiplier on Spider, Coconut, or Pineapple.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Activate Active Consumables">
    Step onto your boosted field and activate a **Super Smoothie** or a combination of **Purple Potions** and **Glitter**. Set your **Gumdrops** to auto-use (or use a macro/hotkey) to ensure the field is constantly covered in goo, which triggers your **Gummy Star** passive.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Execute Triangulate Patterns">
    As your Vector Bees spawn Mark tokens, physically walk to position yourself so that you link these marks together. Stand inside the resulting **Triangulate triangles** to collect massive amounts of pollen. Use **Micro-Converters** immediately when your backpack fills up to keep farming without leaving the field.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Optimize Your Field Positioning">
During a boost, always watch for the Gummy Morph trigger. When active, prioritize standing in the dense center of the field where your Fuzzy Bees have upgraded the flowers. This maximizes the raw pollen input that your Vector Bees convert into honey.
</Callout>

</Section>
