<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Star Amulet Guide: Unlocking Supreme Star Amulet Passives">

The Star Amulet is the most powerful progression gate in the game. Located in the Star Hall above the No-Bee Gate, this amulet scales directly with the number of unique gifted bee types discovered in your hive. Unlike normal bees, gifted bees provide a permanent hive-wide bonus that does not stack with duplicate gifted bees of the same type.

To upgrade your Star Amulet, you must discover unique gifted types and pay a designated honey cost. The table below details the requirements for each tier.

| Tier | Gifted Bees Required | Cost in Honey | Key Guaranteed Stats |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Bronze Star Amulet | 5 Unique Gifted | 250,000 | +10% Capacity, x1.1 Pollen |
| Silver Star Amulet | 10 Unique Gifted | 10,000,000 | +25% Capacity, x1.2 Pollen |
| Gold Star Amulet | 20 Unique Gifted | 100,000,000 | +50% Capacity, x1.3 Pollen, 1 Passive |
| Diamond Star Amulet | 30 Unique Gifted | 1,000,000,000 | +100% Capacity, x1.4 Pollen, 1 Passive |
| Supreme Star Amulet (SSA) | 40 Unique Gifted | 10,000,000,000 | +150% Capacity, x1.5 Pollen, 1-2 Passives |

<Callout type="tip" title="Gifted Discovery Tracking">
You do not need to keep all 40 unique gifted bees in your active hive simultaneously to unlock the Supreme Star Amulet. The game tracks your "discovered" gifted types in your badge menu. Once a gifted type is discovered, you can safely royal jelly over it if it does not fit your final hive build.
</Callout>

Once you unlock the Supreme Star Amulet (SSA), you gain access to powerful passive abilities. These passives are the foundation of late-game hive specialization. Landing the correct passive determines whether your hive should focus on Red, Blue, or White pollen.

| Passive Name | Effect Summary | Best Hive Color |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Pop Star | Spawns bubbles that collect blue pollen and multiply capacity | Blue Hive |
| Scorching Star | Spawns fireflies that boost red pollen and create fire fields | Red Hive |
| Gummy Star | Spawns gumdrops and triggers massive goo-collecting boosts | White Hive |
| Guiding Star | Creates a beacon in your least-farmed field for massive multipliers | Mixed Hive |
| Star Shower | Drops stars that collect pollen and restore player energy | Mixed / Blue Hive |
| Star Saw | Summons a saw that converts pollen and clears cobwebs | Red / White Hive |

<Section icon={Target} title="Amulet Stat Optimization: Rerolling Strategies">

Rerolling your amulets is a necessary step to transition from mid-game to endgame. However, doing so inefficiently can drain your resources. The Moon Shrine, located behind the Bamboo Field, is the perfect place to practice amulet optimization before tackling the high-cost Star Amulet rerolls.

Follow these steps to safely upgrade and optimize your Moon Amulet stats without wasting your hard-earned Moon Charms.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Collect Moon Charms">
    Gather Moon Charms by defeating Fireflies at night, completing King Beetle fights, playing the Moon Memory Match, or turning in quests to Spirit Bear. You will need at least 100 Moon Charms for your first roll.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Visit the Moon Shrine">
    Travel to the Moon Shrine located next to the 10-bee gate. Stand in the generator circle to open the amulet crafting interface.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Generate and Compare Stats">
    Spend your Moon Charms to generate a new Moon Amulet. The game will display your current amulet stats alongside the newly rolled stats. Check for high-priority modifiers like Capacity, Bond from Treats, and Honey per Pollen.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Decide to Keep or Discard">
    If the new roll is superior, accept the replacement. If it is worse, discard it. Your next rolls will cost fewer Moon Charms, floor-capping at 50 charms per roll once you have generated your fifth amulet.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Supreme Star Amulet Rerolling Danger">
Rerolling a Supreme Star Amulet costs a flat 10 Billion honey per attempt. Chasing a perfect "double passive" roll (such as Pop Star + Star Shower) can easily bankrupt your hive. Secure a strong single passive with matching color stats first before attempting double passive rolls.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Challenge Amulet Guide: Ant Arena and Stick Bug Tactics">

Unlike the Star and Moon amulets, which are purchased with static resources, the Ant Amulet and Stick Bug Amulet are earned through active combat challenges. Your score in these challenges directly dictates the tier of the amulet you receive.

The Ant Challenge requires an Ant Pass to enter. You must defeat waves of ants within a strict time limit. The table below shows the score thresholds required to secure higher-tier Ant Amulets.

| Tier | Score Required | Key Stats |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Bronze Ant Amulet | 1 - 49 Points | +Player Movespeed, +Pollen |
| Silver Ant Amulet | 50 - 99 Points | +Critical Chance, +Blue/Red Pollen |
| Gold Ant Amulet | 100 - 149 Points | +Critical Power, +White Pollen |
| Diamond Ant Amulet | 150 - 249 Points | +Convert Rate, +Attack, +Loot Luck |
| Supreme Ant Amulet | 250+ Points | Max stat rolls, +Extra Bee Attack |

The Stick Bug Challenge is an even more demanding battle. Triggered by interacting with the Stick Bug NPC or using a Stick Bug Totem, this event scales in difficulty based on your hive level. 

| Tier | Score Required | Key Stats |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Bronze Stick Bug Amulet | 1,000,000 - 4,999,999 | +Shared Luck, +Red/Blue Pollen |
| Silver Stick Bug Amulet | 5,000,000 - 14,999,999 | +Bee Attack, +Pollen from Tools |
| Gold Stick Bug Amulet | 15,000,000 - 49,999,999 | +Critical Chance, +Capacity |
| Diamond Stick Bug Amulet | 50,000,000 - 99,999,999 | +Extra Red/Blue Pollen, +Loot Luck |
| Supreme Stick Bug Amulet | 100,000,000+ | Max stats, +Massive Shared Luck |
