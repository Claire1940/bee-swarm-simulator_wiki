<Section icon={Map} title="How to Farm Aphids Effectively">

Farming **bee swarm simulator aphids** requires a mix of patience and field management. Since they spawn from leaves, the more pollen you collect, the higher your chances. However, there is a hidden mechanic: the "Field Absence" bonus.

| Strategy | Method | Pros | Cons |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Field Hopping** | Move between fields every 5 minutes | Higher spawn triggers | Disrupts nectar building |
| **Leaf Grinding** | Stay in one field with high haste | Constant spawn attempts | Lower "Absence" bonus |
| **Server Hopping** | Check high-level fields in new servers | Find existing spawns | High loading times |

The most effective way to force a spawn is to avoid a field for at least 15-20 minutes. When you finally enter and begin collecting, the game calculates a higher probability for an aphid to appear in the first few sets of leaves you rustle.
