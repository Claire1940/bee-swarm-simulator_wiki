<Section icon={Trophy} title="Hive Balloon Conversion and Blessing Scaling">

When a balloon is fully filled or its time limit expires, it automatically travels back to your hive and merges into the "Hive Balloon" [Balloon]. The Hive Balloon floats in place above your hive slot, holding its stored pollen until you begin the conversion process [Balloon]. Your bees will always prioritize converting the contents of your backpack before they begin converting the pollen stored inside the Hive Balloon [Balloon].

Converting this accumulated pollen grants the highly coveted Balloon Blessing buff, which provides massive multipliers to both your overall Capacity and your Honey at Hive rate [Balloon].

The pollen stored inside your Hive Balloon is not permanent and will gradually deplete over time [Balloon]. At its slowest, the balloon shrinks by 20% per hour; however, if the balloon contains over 100 times your maximum capacity, it will deflate rapidly, losing up to 99.99% of its pollen within 10 minutes [Balloon].
