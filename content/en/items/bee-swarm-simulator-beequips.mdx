<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Optimization Strategy">

To get the most out of your **bee swarm simulator beequips**, you should follow a structured waxing path. This minimizes the waste of rare materials like Swirl Wax while ensuring your bees receive the highest possible stat boosts.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Stat Re-rolling">
    Before applying any level-increasing wax, use Swirl Wax to roll for the specific stats your hive needs (e.g., Blue Pollen, Critical Chance). It is much cheaper to re-roll a Level 0 item than an upgraded one.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Caustic Gamble">
    If the Beequip has "Caustic Only" abilities, apply Caustic Wax first. If the item survives and unlocks the ability, you can proceed. If it fails, you haven't wasted other waxes on it yet.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Hard Wax Progression">
    Apply Hard Wax for the next 3-5 levels. Statistically, you can expect a 60% success rate, which often yields a +6 Power Level gain over five attempts.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Soft Wax Polishing">
    Once you reach a high Power Level where the risk of failure becomes too costly, switch to Soft Wax to finish the remaining levels safely. This "Hybrid Method" is the meta standard for 2026.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Strategy Name | Recommended For | Wax Combination |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **The Safe Bet** | Early game / Commons | 100% Soft Wax |
| **The Professional** | Mid-game / Rares | 3 Hard Wax + 2 Soft Wax |
| **The High Roller** | Endgame / Mythics | 1 Swirl + 1 Caustic + 4 Hard Wax |
| **The Reroller** | Specific Builds | Infinite Swirl until perfect stats |

<Callout type="info" title="Optimization Tip">
If you successfully land two Hard Waxes in your first three attempts, switching to Soft Wax for the final two slots guarantees a total Power Level increase of +6 while eliminating all remaining risk.
</Callout>
