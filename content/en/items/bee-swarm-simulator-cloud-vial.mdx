<Section icon={Target} title="The Windy Bee Summoning Process">

The most common reason players farm Cloud Vials is to obtain the **Windy Bee**. This process is governed by a hidden mechanic called "Favor." Every item you donate to the Wind Shrine increases your favor, and once your favor is high enough, donating Cloud Vials has a chance to trigger the "Windy Bee Egg" reward.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Donate a Spirit Petal">
    You must donate one of your three Spirit Petals to the Wind Shrine. Most pro players recommend donating your **second** petal, using the first for the Petal Wand.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Build Favor">
    Donate items like Cloud Vials, Star Jelly, or Stickers to increase your hidden favor score. Higher favor increases the probability of the egg drop.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Spam Cloud Vials">
    Once your favor is established, donate Cloud Vials in stacks of 10. Each vial in the stack acts as an individual roll for the Windy Bee egg.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Check Dialogue">
    The Wind Shrine's text responses change as your favor grows. Look for phrases indicating the wind is "pleased" or "intrigued."
  </Step>
</Steps>
