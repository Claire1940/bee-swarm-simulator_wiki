**Passive Ability: X-Flame Mechanics**
The X-Flame ability is a game-changer for red hives. Every 25th battle ability token you collect triggers the following:
- Spawns **29 flames** in an "X" shape around your character.
- Each flame lasts for **3 seconds**.
- Flames collect 4 Red, 2 White, and 1 Blue pollen every second.
- Deals **15 damage** per second to any enemy touching the flames.
- Grants **Flame Heat**, which further boosts red pollen and bee attack power.
