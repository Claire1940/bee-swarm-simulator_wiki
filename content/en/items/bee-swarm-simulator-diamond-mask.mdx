<Section icon={Swords} title="Material Farming Strategies">

Beyond the eggs, you need a massive supply of Blue Extracts and Glitter. Efficiency is key here; blindly grinding will take twice as long as a targeted strategy.

**Blue Extract Farming**
The best way to gather the 12,500 Blueberries needed is to utilize the Blue Dispenser every 4 hours and focus on Bucko Bee's quests. Bucko Bee frequently rewards Blue Extracts directly, bypassing the need for crafting. Additionally, participating in "Sprout Parties" in blue fields like Pine Tree Forest will yield a high volume of Blueberries.

**Glitter and Oil Farming**
Glitter is best obtained by farming Moon Charms during the night cycle. Use the Night Memory Match consistently. For Oil, planting your Tacky Planter or Pesticide Planter in the Sunflower Field guarantees a steady stream of Sunflower Seeds.

| Material | Best Field | Mob to Kill | Memory Match |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Blueberries** | Pine Tree Forest | Rhino Beetle | Mega Match |
| **Sunflower Seeds** | Sunflower Field | Ladybug | Extreme Match |
| **Moon Charms** | Any (Night Only) | Werewolf | Night Match |
| **Magic Beans** | Stump Field | King Beetle | Supreme Match |

<Section icon={Zap} title="Passive Ability: Diamond Drain">

The true power of the **bee swarm simulator diamond mask** lies in its passive: **Diamond Drain**. This ability is what makes Blue Hives the kings of "macroing" and long-term farming sessions.

**How Diamond Drain Works:**
Every 30th Blue Ability Token collected triggers the effect. It instantly converts 5,000,000 Pollen plus a percentage of your total Bee Convert Amount. This conversion is further boosted by:
- +10% per Blue Bee in your hive.
- +50% per Gifted Blue Bee type.

Additionally, it pollinates 10 blue flowers (plus 3 more per blue bee) in the immediate vicinity. This creates a feedback loop where your flowers stay at maximum growth, allowing for sustained high-pollen collection without depleting the field.

| Feature | Impact on Gameplay |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Instant Conversion** | Prevents bag overfilling during boosts |
| **Pollination** | Keeps blue fields at Tier 3 growth |
| **Hive Synergy** | Rewards players for specializing in Blue Bees |
| **Cooldown** | 45 Seconds (Balanced for high-speed play) |

</Callout>
