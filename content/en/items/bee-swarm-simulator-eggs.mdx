<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Step-by-Step Guide to Managing Your Eggs">

To maximize your hive's efficiency and avoid wasting rare resources, follow this structured management pipeline whenever you obtain new eggs.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Claim an Empty Hive Slot">
    Before using any egg, ensure you have purchased an empty hive slot using Honey at the Mountain Top Shop or the starter area. Hatching an egg on an occupied slot will permanently overwrite the existing bee.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Check Your Current Gear Goals">
    Before dragging an egg onto your hive, verify your progress toward mid-game and late-game masks. If you are planning to build a Blue Hive, you must check your Diamond Egg inventory immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Decide: Hatch, Craft, or Donate">
    Apply the golden rule of egg management: Basic and Silver eggs are safe to hatch or donate. Gold eggs should be saved for Honey Masks or late-game boosts. Diamond eggs must be hoarded for the Diamond Mask.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute with Royal Jelly Alternatives">
    If your goal is simply to get a Legendary bee, do not waste a Diamond Egg. Instead, buy Royal Jellies in bulk and use the "Auto-Jelly until Legendary" setting on a Basic Bee slot. This preserves your rare eggs for crafting.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Warning">
Never hatch your first five Diamond Eggs. The Diamond Mask requires exactly 5 Diamond Eggs to craft. If you hatch them, you will be forced to grind 2,000 Tickets or rely on incredibly low boss drop rates later in your progression.
</Callout>
