The performance of the **bee swarm simulator electro magnet** is defined by its unique "Surround" pattern. <Section icon={Sparkles} title="Trivia and Hidden Facts">

The **bee swarm simulator electro magnet** is more than just a tool; it's a piece of the game's history. Being one of the few tools added in a specific content update (June 2018), it filled a gap in the progression curve that previously felt too steep.
