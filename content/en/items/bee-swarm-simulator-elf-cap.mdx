<Section icon={Swords} title="How to Upgrade and Optimize Your Elf Cap">

While you cannot obtain a new **bee swarm simulator elf cap** from drops, you can still use the Wax system to improve an existing one. Using Soft, Hard, or Caustic Wax can increase the stats of your beequip, but it also consumes its upgrade slots.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Assessment">
    Check the current potential and remaining upgrade slots. If the cap has 0 slots left, it cannot be improved further without specific specialized items.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Soft Waxing">
    Use Soft Wax for low-risk improvements. This is best for 0-2 star caps where you want to nudge the stats higher without risking a slot on a failed high-tier wax.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Hard Waxing">
    Hard Wax offers a better chance at significant stat increases but has a higher failure rate. Use this for 3-star caps to push them toward 4-star territory.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Caustic Wax Application">
    Caustic Wax is the high-stakes option. It can provide massive boosts to Convert Rate at Hive but risks destroying the upgrade slot or providing a mediocre roll.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Waxing Caution">
Never use Caustic Wax on a 5-star Elf Cap unless you are an absolute end-game player with resources to spare. The risk of "bricking" a high-value collectible is significant.
</Callout>
