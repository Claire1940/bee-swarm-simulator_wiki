<Section icon={Map} title="Understanding Bee Swarm Simulator Field Dice Mechanics">

In the world of Onett's creation, **bee swarm simulator field dice** serve as a fundamental inventory item for players looking to optimize their pollen collection. Originally added in the 2019 update and refined in late 2021, these items are essential for mid-game and end-game progression. When consumed, a Field Die (singular) selects a random field that the player has unlocked and applies a massive 100% Pollen Field Boost. This effect lasts for 15 minutes, providing a significant window for high-intensity farming.

Understanding the limitations of these items is just as important as knowing their benefits. While the boost is powerful, it is crucial to remember that Field Dice do not stack their duration or intensity. If you roll a die while a boost is already active, the new roll will completely replace the previous one. This means you should always wait for your 15-minute timer to expire—or nearly expire—before rolling again, unless you are specifically fishing for a different field boost.

| Dice Type | Boost Percentage | Duration | Main Source |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Field Dice** | +100% Pollen | 15 Minutes | Mobs, Quests, Planters |
| **Smooth Dice** | +100% Pollen + extra | 15 Minutes | Blender Crafting |
| **Loaded Dice** | +100% (Multiple Fields) | 15 Minutes | Robo Bear, Special Packs |

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Advanced Strategies: Planters and Puffshrooms">

For the dedicated 2026 player, passive income of **bee swarm simulator field dice** through Planters is the gold standard. Planters like the Tacky Planter and the Petal Planter have specific field affinities that increase the likelihood of receiving Dice when harvested.

- **Tacky Planter**: Best used in the Clover or Sunflower fields for basic material drops.
- **Petal Planter**: Ideally placed in the Pine Tree or Pumpkin fields for high-tier rewards including multiple Dice.
- **Puffshrooms**: While rare, defeating Rare or Epic Puffshrooms can yield Field Dice. This is most effective when done in a group to clear fields faster.

Planters require patience, as they take several hours of active play (or longer offline) to fully grow. However, the reward density is much higher than standard mob farming. If you are aiming for a specific field boost, having a stockpile of 50+ Dice is recommended before starting a "boost cycle" where you roll until you hit your desired field.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Crafting and Wind Shrine Utility">

Once you have accumulated a surplus of **bee swarm simulator field dice**, you can begin using them for advanced crafting or summoning powerful entities. The Blender, located in the 10-bee zone, allows you to transmute basic Dice into more powerful variants.

**Crafting Smooth Dice:**
To craft a Smooth Die, which provides a more stable and often superior boost, you will need the following ingredients in the Blender:
- 3 Field Dice
- 3 Soft Waxes
- 3 Whirligigs
- 3 Oils

Beyond crafting, the Wind Shrine is a vital mechanic for Field Dice. Donating exactly 3 Field Dice to the Shrine has a nearly 100% success rate for summoning a Wild Windy Bee. This is the most efficient way to farm Cloud Vials and Windy Bee tokens without waiting for natural spawns.
