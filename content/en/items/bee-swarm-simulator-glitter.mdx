<Section icon={Swords} title="Daily Boss Rotations & Loot Luck">

Consistently defeating bosses is the backbone of any successful farming routine. Bosses have dedicated loot tables with high chances of dropping Glitter, but you must manage their respawn timers strictly. To maximize your efficiency, always trigger the **Baby Love** buff (granted by Baby Bee or Gifted Tadpole Bee) right before delivering the final blow. This active buff boosts your **Loot Luck by 50%**, significantly increasing the probability of a Glitter drop.

| Boss | Cooldown | Drop Chance | Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Tunnel Bear** | 48 Hours | Very High | Always defeat with Baby Love active |
| **King Beetle** | 24 Hours | Medium-High | Quick daily kill, easy to access |
| **Coconut Crab** | 36 Hours | Medium | Requires high-level bees to defeat quickly |
| **Stump Snail** | 96 Hours | Low-Medium | Best suited for overnight AFK farming |

Beyond these standard bosses, the **Wild Windy Bee** is another excellent target. Defeating a Windy Bee yields a fair amount of Glitter, especially at higher levels. 

<Callout type="tip" title="Windy Bee Summoning">
You can summon a Wild Windy Bee at the Wind Shrine by donating a single **Star Jelly**, three **Glue**, or three **Magic Beans**. While the shrine is RNG-dependent, it is a highly viable active combat option.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Map} title="Nighttime Farming & Memory Match Strategies">

When night falls, the map undergoes changes that make farming much easier. Fireflies descend from the sky, leaving behind tokens that occasionally contain Glitter. More importantly, nighttime is the optimal window to summon **Moon Sprouts**. Breaking these sprouts rewards you with Moon Charms and Magic Beans, which are the exact ingredients needed for the Blender recipe.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Wait for Nighttime">
    Monitor the in-game clock and prepare to move to fields once darkness falls.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Summon Moon Sprouts">
    Use Magic Beans in fields during the night. Special sprouts have a high chance of turning into Moon Sprouts.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Collect Firefly Tokens">
    Run through the glowing firefly circles to collect Moon Charms and rare Glitter drops.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Play Nighttime Memory Match">
    Head to the 30 Bee Zone and complete the Memory Match game before daytime returns.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Memory Matches are cooldown-based minigames that you should treat as daily tasks. While almost all Memory Matches have a chance to reward you, the Nighttime Memory Match is particularly lucrative.

| Memory Match | Cost | Location | Glitter Chance |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Nighttime MM** | Free | 30 Bee Zone (Night) | **Very High** |
| **Extreme MM** | 25,000,000 Honey | 35 Bee Zone | **High** |
| **Pro MM** | 250,000 Honey | Badge Bearer's Guild | **Medium** |
| **Basic MM** | 25,000 Honey | 10 Bee Zone | **Low** |
