<Section icon={Target} title="Gummy Boots Recipe & Crafting Costs">

Crafting the Gummy Boots is one of the most resource-intensive endeavors in the game. Initially, the recipe was even more demanding, requiring 999 Glue and 300 of each extract. However, Onett later balanced the recipe to make it more achievable for late-game players. 

In addition to the raw honey cost, you must gather a vast array of rare crafting materials and specialized nectar vials. This requires long-term planning, consistent daily boss runs, and strategic use of your blender.

| Ingredient | Quantity | Primary Acquisition Method |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Honey** | 100,000,000,000 | Active grinding, field boosts, and boss runs |
| **Glue** | 500 | Glue Dispenser, crafting with gumdrops/royal jelly |
| **Glitter** | 250 | Crafting with moon charms/magic beans, memory match |
| **Red Extract** | 250 | Crafting with strawberries/royal jelly, King Beetle |
| **Blue Extract** | 250 | Crafting with blueberries/royal jelly, Bucko quests |
| **Satisfying Vial** | 1 | Planters in Pine Tree/Rose fields, nectar harvesting |
| **Motivating Vial** | 1 | Planters in Spider/Bamboo fields, nectar harvesting |

To put this recipe into perspective, crafting all the glue, glitter, and extracts from raw materials requires thousands of individual ingredients, including over 16,000 royal jellies and hundreds of magic beans. Without drops from mobs, memory matches, and quest rewards, the raw crafting time in the blender would take over 100 hours of continuous active playtime.

<Callout type="warning" title="Crafting Time">
Do not craft all your materials at once. Utilize your daily blender queues and prioritize crafting Glitter and Glue whenever you have the raw materials, as these are the biggest bottlenecks in the recipe.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Map} title="How to Grind for Gummy Boots: Step-by-Step">

Gathering the resources for this late-game gear requires a structured daily routine. If you try to farm everything at once, you will quickly find yourself overwhelmed by the sheer volume of materials required. Follow this step-by-step farming routine to optimize your progression.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Daily Glue Collection">
    Claim free Glue from the Glue Dispenser in the 10-Bee Zone daily. The amount of Glue you receive scales directly with your Goo Badge tier. Focus on leveling up your Goo Badge early to maximize this daily source.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Blender Optimization">
    Keep your blender running constantly. Prioritize crafting Glitter using Moon Charms and Magic Beans, and craft Red and Blue Extracts using Blueberries, Strawberries, and Royal Jellies. Never let your blender sit idle.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Nectar Cultivation">
    Deploy planters in specific fields to cultivate Satisfying and Motivating Nectars. Harvest them into vials once they reach full maturity. Keeping these nectars active also provides valuable buffs to your pollen collection while you grind.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Final Purchase">
    Accumulate 100 Billion Honey through coordinated field boosts. Once all materials are secured, enter Gummy Bear's Lair via the Gummy Bee teleporter and claim your boots.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Daily Routine">

</Callout>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Unlocking Gummy Boots Passive Abilities">

The true power of the Gummy Boots lies in their two unique passive abilities. Unlike lower-tier boots, these passives operate independently and provide massive boosts to both movement speed and field conversion.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Goo Trail Passive">
    - **Flower Coverage**: Automatically covers 5 surrounding flowers in goo as you walk.
    - **Goo Multipliers**: Works in tandem with the Gummy Mask to maximize white field farming.
    - **Zero Cost**: Passive operates continuously without consuming gumdrops.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Coconut Haste Passive">
    - **Trigger**: Activates upon catching a falling coconut from the Coconut Canister.
    - **Haste Boost**: Grants a stack of Haste and a 2-second Coconut Haste Surge.
    - **Stat Multipliers**: Provides x1.5 Bee Movespeed and +10 Player Movespeed.
  </Card>

The Goo Trail passive originally only covered a single flower directly beneath the player, but Onett later buffed it to cover the nearest five flowers, making it incredibly potent for maintaining high goo multipliers across entire fields. Meanwhile, the Coconut Haste passive ensures that your bees move incredibly fast, allowing them to gather and convert pollen at unprecedented rates during active boosts.

<Callout type="tip" title="No Inheritance Required">
You do not need to purchase or own the Coconut Clogs to benefit from the Coconut Haste passive on the Gummy Boots. The boots inherently possess both passives, saving you from spending extra resources on mid-game footwear if you choose to skip them.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Progression Guide: Gummy Boots vs. Supreme Star Amulet">

A common debate among late-game players is whether to prioritize saving 100 Billion Honey for the Gummy Boots or spending it on obtaining a Supreme Star Amulet (SSA). While the boots offer incredible stats, rushing them before securing an SSA is generally considered a progression trap.

An SSA unlocks game-changing passives like Pop Star, Shower, or Gummy Star, which completely redefine your honey generation. Once you have an active SSA passive tailored to your hive color, earning 100 Billion Honey becomes a matter of a few successful boosts rather than weeks of slow grinding.

| Milestone | Recommended Status | Why It Matters |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Gummy Mask** | Required | Essential for maximizing the value of Goo Trail |
| **Coconut Canister** | Highly Recommended | Needed to spawn falling coconuts for Coconut Haste |
| **SSA (Supreme Star Amulet)** | Recommended | Drastically speeds up the 100 Billion Honey grind |
| **Level 12+ Hive** | Recommended | Ensures efficient mob clearing and material farming |

Before you commit all your hard-earned materials and honey to the Gummy Bear's Lair, ensure your hive is fully prepared to utilize these boots to their maximum potential.
