<Section icon={Swords} title="Weapon Tier List and Comparison">

In the Retro Swarm Challenge, your sword is your primary tool for brick generation. Bricks are dropped by mobs and found in brick blooms; however, killing mobs quickly is the fastest way to accumulate wealth. You must upgrade your sword in a specific sequence to maintain your "one-shot" capability as mob health scales.

| Weapon Name | Brick Cost | Damage Output | Best Usage Period |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Classic Sword** | 100 Bricks | Low | Rounds 1-2 (Initial clearing) |
| **Firebrand** | 300 Bricks | ~200 Damage | Rounds 3-5 (Zombies/Slimes) |
| **Illumina** | 1,500 Bricks | ~1,000 Damage | Rounds 6-15 (Elite mobs) |

<Callout type="tip" title="Hotbar Optimization">
Always drag your new sword into the **first slot** of your hotbar. This allows you to switch instantly between your collection tool and your weapon using number keys, ensuring you don't miss a single mob spawn.
</Callout>

While the Firebrand is a significant upgrade over the Classic Sword, it is merely a stepping stone. You should save your bricks aggressively after getting the Firebrand to reach the 1,500 required for the Illumina as soon as possible.

<Section icon={Target} title="Optimal Buying Order Walkthrough">

Following a strict purchasing sequence is the difference between failing at Round 10 and reaching Round 20. Many players make the mistake of buying too many bees early on, which slows down their ability to acquire the **bee swarm simulator illumina**.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Three-Bee Start">
    Immediately buy your first 3 bees. Do not buy any more until much later. These bees provide just enough support to clear Round 1 while you focus on breaking brick blooms.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Sword Progression">
    Buy the Classic Sword (100 bricks) followed by the Firebrand (300 bricks). Use the Firebrand to farm zombies in Round 3, which are the easiest source of bricks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Illumina Rush">
    Save 1,500 bricks. Do not spend on anything else. Once you equip the Illumina, you can solo clear most mobs, allowing your teammates to focus on their own upgrades.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Buff and Utility Phase">
    After Illumina, purchase the four food items (Bloxy A, Cola, Cheeseburger, Pizza) in that order. The Pizza is the most expensive but provides the highest attack buff.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Defensive Transition">
    Buy the Brick Trowel. Start placing walls between rounds to stall mobs. Only now should you spend all remaining bricks on maxing out your bee count.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Early Bee Traps">
Buying bees before you have the Illumina is a "trap" strategy. Bees are expensive and their damage scaling in the early rounds is inferior to the raw power of the Illumina sword.
</Callout>

</Section>
