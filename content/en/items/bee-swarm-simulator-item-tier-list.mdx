<Section icon={Target} title="How to Progress Your Gear Order">

Following a strict upgrade path is the best way to avoid wasting resources. Use these steps to move through the **bee swarm simulator item tier list** efficiently.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Early Game Foundation">
    Focus on the Pro Shop items. Get the **Golden Rake** and the **Red/Blue Port-O-Hives**. Start your Black Bear and Mother Bear quests immediately to earn honey and tickets.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Mountain Top Push">
    Save every drop of honey for the **Porcelain Dipper** and **Porcelain Port-O-Hive**. This duo will carry you through 70% of the game's core content.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Ace Shop & Spirit Bear">
    Once you have 35 bees, enter the 35-bee zone. Craft the **Honeycomb Belt** and start working on Spirit Bear quests for your first **Spirit Petal** to get the **Petal Wand**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Specialization & Endgame">
    Choose a hive color (Blue is recommended for beginners) and craft the corresponding mask (Diamond). Work toward the **Coconut Canister** and eventually your endgame collector.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Common Mistake">
Many players buy the Fire or Bubble mask too early. Unless you are strictly focusing on one color for quests, save your materials for the **Honey Mask** or **Gummy Mask** instead.
</Callout>
