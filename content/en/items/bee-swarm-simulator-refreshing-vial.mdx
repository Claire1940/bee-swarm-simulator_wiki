<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Farming Refreshing Nectar with Planters">

To use the Nectar Condenser, you must first accumulate Refreshing Nectar. This nectar type is generated by placing planters in specific fields. The rate at which you gather nectar depends heavily on the type of planter used and the field it is placed in.

The primary fields that yield Refreshing Nectar are Strawberry, Coconut, Clover, and Blue Flower. Using compatible planters in these locations will significantly accelerate your farming process.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Step-by-Step Nectar Condenser Guide">

The Nectar Condenser is the most reliable machine for turning raw nectar into usable vials. Located inside Dapper Bear's shop, this machine requires a substantial amount of accumulated nectar to function.

Follow these steps to successfully condense your gathered nectar into vials:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Accumulate Nectar">
    Harvest at least 12 hours of Refreshing Nectar from your planters. You can track your current nectar levels in the system menu.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Visit Dapper Bear">
    Travel to Dapper Bear's shop located near the 30-Bee Gate. Make sure you have empty inventory slots for the vial.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Interact with Condenser">
    Approach the Nectar Condenser machine and select the Refreshing Nectar option to begin the condensation process.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Collect Your Vial">
    Confirm the trade of 12 hours of nectar for 1 **bee swarm simulator refreshing vial**. The item will be added directly to your inventory.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Using the condenser regularly is key to maintaining a healthy stockpile. If you macro or play actively for long sessions, make it a habit to visit the condenser at the end of each session to convert your active nectar hours before they decay.

<Callout type="info" title="Condenser Cooldown">
The Nectar Condenser does not have a strict daily cooldown, but it is limited by how quickly you can farm the required 12 hours of nectar.
</Callout>
