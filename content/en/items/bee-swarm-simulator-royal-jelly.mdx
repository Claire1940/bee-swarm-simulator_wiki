<Section icon={Sparkles} title="What is Royal Jelly in Bee Swarm Simulator?">

In Bee Swarm Simulator, Royal Jelly is one of the most critical progression resources. When applied to any bee in your hive, it transforms that bee into a random bee of at least **Rare** rarity. This mechanic is the primary way players upgrade their starter hives, discover new bee types, and eventually build specialized color builds (Red, Blue, or White hives).

Understanding the mathematical odds of Royal Jelly rolls is essential to avoid wasting this precious resource. The table below outlines the exact probabilities when manual rolling or auto-rolling a single Royal Jelly.

| Bee Rarity | Roll Probability | Average Cost (in Jellies) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Rare** | **70.00%** | 1.4 |
| **Epic** | **27.00%** | 3.7 |
| **Legendary** | **3.00%** | 33.3 |
| **Mythic** | **0.004%** | 25,000 |
| **Gifted (Any)** | **0.40%** | 250 |

<Callout type="warning" title="Auto-Roll Warning">
Always configure your auto-roll settings before spamming Royal Jelly on a slot. Accidentally rolling over a Mythic or a rare Gifted bee because of manual clicking is a common mistake that can set back your progression by weeks.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Map} title="How to Farm Royal Jelly Fast">

Acquiring **bee swarm simulator royal jelly** in large quantities changes as you progress from the early game to the endgame. While beginners must rely on quests and field exploration, advanced players buy them by the tens of thousands using honey. 

The table below highlights the primary sources of Royal Jelly, their locations, and their overall efficiency for active players.

| Source Type | Location / Requirements | Cost / Cooldown | Efficiency Rating |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Royal Jelly Dispenser** | 10-Bee Zone (near Slime) | 6 Tickets per Jelly | Low (Expensive early-game) |
| **Bulk Dispenser** | Mountain Top Shop | Honey (Scales with purchase) | High (Best for late-game) |
| **Blender Crafting** | 15-Bee Zone | 10,000 Honey, 10 of each seed | Medium (Resource dependent) |
| **King Beetle Boss** | Blue Flower Field Cave | 30-Hour Cooldown | High (Great passive daily drop) |

<Callout type="tip" title="Ticket Conservation Tip">
Avoid spending your hard-earned Tickets on the Royal Jelly Dispenser in the early game. Tickets are far more valuable when saved for Event Bees like the Tabby Bee, Photon Bee, and Crimson/Cobalt Bees.
</Callout>

For active farming, focus on defeating mobs on their exact spawn cooldowns. Mobs like the Werewolf (in the Pumpkin Patch cave) and the Spider (near the Strawberry Field) have high drop rates for Royal Jellies and crafting materials.

<Section icon={Swords} title="Step-by-Step Royal Jelly Progression Strategy">

Using your Royal Jellies efficiently determines how fast you can progress through the different stages of Bee Swarm Simulator. Follow this structured progression path to maximize your resources.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Early-Game Hoard (1 to 25 Bees)">
    During this phase, do not spend your Royal Jellies to roll for specific bees. Keep a diverse hive of basic, rare, and epic bees. Save every single Royal Jelly you find from quests, hidden tokens, and mob drops. Your goal is to accumulate at least **200 Royal Jellies** before starting any major crafting projects.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Mid-Game Gifted Hunt (25 to 40 Bees)">
    Once you unlock the 30-Bee Zone and start working toward the **Gold Star Amulet**, you need unique Gifted bee types. Use the auto-roll feature set to **Require Gifted**. Roll individual slots containing duplicate normal bees until you land a new unique Gifted type. Do not roll over existing unique Gifted bonuses.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Crafting the Petal Wand and Guards (35+ Bees)">
    At this stage, redirect your Royal Jelly stock entirely into the Blender. You will need hundreds of jellies to craft the **Enzymes** and **Oil** required for the Crimson Guard, Cobalt Guard, and the Petal Wand. Stop rolling for bees temporarily and focus entirely on gear upgrades.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Late-Game Mythic Transition (45+ Bees)">
    When you are ready to transition to a specialized Red, Blue, or White hive, you will need to buy Royal Jellies in massive bulk from the Mountain Top dispenser using honey. Expect to spend **billions of honey** to secure the necessary Mythic bees (such as Buoyant Bees for Blue hives or Spicy Bees for Red hives) using the **Until Mythic** auto-roll setting.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Mythic Roll Milestone">
On average, rolling a Mythic bee requires 25,000 Royal Jellies, which costs **25 billion honey**. Do not attempt to roll for Mythics until your daily honey production can easily support this scale of spending.
</Callout>
