<Section icon={Map} title="Introduction to the Sticker Seeker System">

The **bee swarm simulator sticker seeker** is a specialized NPC located behind the 15-Bee Gate, positioned near the Pineapple Patch and the Pro Shop. Introduced as a core part of the game's expansion into collectibles and trading, this character serves as the gateway to the Sticker system. Unlike traditional bears that focus solely on pollen collection, the Sticker Seeker tasks players with environmental puzzles, item discovery, and the management of the Sticker Stack.

Stickers in Bee Swarm Simulator are more than just cosmetic flair for your hive. They provide essential buffs, serve as quest requirements for late-game NPCs like Spirit Bear, and form the basis of the player-driven economy in the Sticker Hub. Understanding how to interact with the Seeker and his associated machinery is vital for any player moving from the mid-game into elite progression.

| Feature | Location | Primary Function |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Sticker Seeker NPC** | 15-Bee Zone | Assigns discovery quests and rewards tools |
| **Sticker Stack** | Near Ticket Tent | Permanent hive buffs via sticker consumption |
| **Sticker Printer** | 15-Bee Zone | Converts Eggs/Items into random stickers |
| **Sticker Hub** | Via Hub Cannon | Dedicated trading zone and community area |

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="The Sticker Stack and Hive Buffs">

One of the most powerful aspects of the **bee swarm simulator sticker seeker** ecosystem is the Sticker Stack. Located near the Ticket Tent, this altar allows you to "donate" stickers to a permanent collection. Each unique sticker added to the stack increases your "Stack Tier," which provides escalating bonuses to pollen collection and hive capacity.

| Stack Tier | Pollen Bonus | Capacity Bonus | Special Perk |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Tier 1-10** | +1% per level | +2,500 per level | Basic white pollen boost |
| **Tier 11-30** | +2% per level | +10,000 per level | Field-specific boosts unlock |
| **Tier 31-50** | +3% per level | +50,000 per level | Increased nectar efficiency |
| **Tier 50+** | +5% per level | +250,000 per level | Ultimate Stack Glow effect |

<Section icon={Swords} title="Using the Sticker Printer Effectively">

By feeding the printer specific items—most commonly Eggs—you receive a random sticker in return. This is the primary method for obtaining high-tier "Mythic" or "Event" stickers.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Sticker Hub and Trading Economy">

The Sticker Hub is a dedicated sub-map accessible via the large yellow cannon in the main world. This area is the heart of the **bee swarm simulator sticker seeker** community, where players can display their collections and trade with others.

**Trading Rules and Etiquette:**
1. **Fair Value**: Most players use "Star Signs" as a baseline currency for high-value trades.
2. **Quest Items**: Stickers required for current seasonal events (like Beesmas) skyrocket in value; trade them early if you don't need them.
3. **Scam Prevention**: Always double-check the items in the trade window before clicking "Accept." Ensure the sticker name matches exactly what you need.

| Sticker Category | Trading Value | Use Case |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Field Stickers** | Low | Sticker Stack fodder |
| **Bee Stickers** | Medium | Hive decoration and specific bear quests |
| **Star Signs** | Very High | Universal currency for elite trading |
| **Animated/Glow** | High | Cosmetic prestige and high-tier stacks |

<Callout type="warning" title="Quest Lock">
Some stickers are "Quest Locked," meaning they cannot be traded until you have completed certain tasks for the Sticker Seeker. Check the item description in your Sticker Book for the 'Untradeable' tag.
</Callout>
