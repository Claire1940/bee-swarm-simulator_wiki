<Section icon={Shield} title="Tide Popper vs. Other Endgame Tools">

Understanding where the **bee swarm simulator tide popper** sits in the hierarchy of tools is vital for players deciding between a Red, Blue, or White hive. The Tide Popper is specifically tuned for "Blue Bomb" and "Bubble" mechanics, making it vastly different from the Dark Scythe or Gummy Baller.

| Feature | Tide Popper (Blue) | Dark Scythe (Red) | Gummy Baller (White) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Primary Stat** | Blue Pollen / Bubbles | Red Pollen / Flames | Goo / White Pollen |
| **Passive Ability** | Tidal Wave | Dark Scythe Slash | Gummy Morph / Suds |
| **Best Field** | Pine Forest | Pepper Patch | Coconut Field |
| **Honey Cost** | 2.5 Trillion | 2.5 Trillion | 5 Trillion |
| **Conversion** | High (via Bubbles) | Instant (via Flames) | Massive (via Goo) |

The Tide Popper's "Tidal Wave" grows in size based on the number of bubbles popped recently. When it reaches maximum size, it surges across the field, collecting massive amounts of pollen and instantly converting a percentage of it into Honey. This makes it the most "afk-friendly" endgame tool when paired with a macro, as the bubbles do most of the work.

<Callout type="warning" title="Hive Synergy">
