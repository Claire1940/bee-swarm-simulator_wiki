<Section icon={Microscope} title="How to Obtain Translators from Science Bear">

Science Bear is the gatekeeper of linguistic knowledge in Bee Swarm Simulator. To earn your first **bee swarm simulator translator**, you must prove your worth as a field researcher. Science Bear's questline is notoriously difficult because it focuses heavily on collecting specific types of pollen and tokens that require diverse bee abilities.

The progression is divided into stages. You must first complete 16 introductory quests before Science Bear even mentions the possibility of a translator. Once those are finished, the translator quests appear at specific intervals.

| Quest Number | Quest Name | Reward | Key Difficulty |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Quest 21** | Limits of Language | **1st Translator** | High White Pollen requirements |
| **Quest 26** | Beesperanto | **2nd Translator** | Ability token collection |
| **Quest 31** | Epistemological Endeavor | **3rd Translator** | Massive goo and pollen totals |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Research Phase">
    Complete the first 16 quests from Science Bear. These focus on basic field research and will help you level up your bees to roughly level 7-8, which is necessary for the harder tasks ahead.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The First Milestone">
    Reach Quest 21, "Limits of Language." You will need to collect millions of pollen from various fields and gather specific tokens like Link Tokens or Science Bear's own research tokens.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Grind to Completion">
    Continue through Quest 26 and finally Quest 31. Each gap of five quests increases the difficulty exponentially, often requiring you to use specialized equipment like the Gummy Mask or specific bee types to generate enough goo or marks.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Patience Required">
Do not expect to get all three translators quickly. The 31st quest, "Epistemological Endeavor," is considered mid-to-late game content and requires a very strong hive and significant time investment.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Swords} title="The Stick Bug Challenge and Rewards">

Once you use a **bee swarm simulator translator** on the Stick Bug, you gain the ability to initiate the Stick Bug Challenge. This is a wave-based combat event where you must defend against the Stick Bug and its summoned totems.

The challenge is unique because it rewards players based on the total score achieved during the encounter. Higher scores lead to better Stick Bug Amulets, which provide significant boosts to pollen collection and bee attack power.

**Stick Bug Amulet Tiers:**
- **Bronze Amulet**: Requires a score of 1,000,000.
- **Silver Amulet**: Requires a score of 5,000,000.
- **Gold Amulet**: Requires a score of 25,000,000.
- **Diamond Amulet**: Requires a score of 100,000,000.
