<Section icon={Zap} title="Understanding the Bee Swarm Simulator Whirligig">

The **bee swarm simulator whirligig** is a unique utility item first introduced during the Beesmas 2021 update. Modeled after maple tree seeds—often called "helicopters" or "whirligigs" due to their spinning descent—this item serves two primary functions: tactical repositioning and efficiency boosting. When consumed, it instantly warps you and your entire swarm back to your claimed hive.

Beyond simple movement, the Whirligig provides a brief but significant window of increased **Convert Rate**. This makes it an invaluable tool for players who find their bags full in a distant field and want to minimize downtime. Instead of walking back or using a more expensive Micro-Converter, a Whirligig offers a hybrid solution of travel and hive processing speed.

<Section icon={Box} title="Crafting Recipes and Blender Uses">

The **bee swarm simulator whirligig** is not just a utility item; it is a critical component for high-level crafting. If you are looking to manipulate field boosts or optimize your pollen collection, you will need to spend your Whirligigs in the Blender.

Most players use Whirligigs to craft **Field Dice**, which are essential for rerolling field boosts. As you progress, these Field Dice are further refined into **Smooth Dice**, which provide even more powerful buffs.

### Blender Requirements

| Resulting Item | Whirligigs Required | Other Ingredients |
|----------------|---------------------|-------------------|
| **Field Dice** | 1 | 1 Soft Wax, 1 Red Extract, 1 Blue Extract |
| **Smooth Dice** | 3 | 3 Field Dice, 3 Oils, 3 Soft Waxes |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Gather Base Materials">

The **bee swarm simulator whirligig** has several "hidden" uses that experienced players exploit to save time and avoid death. Understanding these interactions can significantly improve your survival rate in high-risk zones.

### Tactical Repositioning

One of the most popular uses for the Whirligig is as an "Escape Rope." In areas where your movement is restricted or death is imminent, the instant teleportation can save your honey and your buffs.

| Scenario | Whirligig Interaction | Benefit |
|----------|-----------------------|---------|
| **White Tunnel** | Teleports you out of the tunnel instantly. | Escape Tunnel Bear without fighting or dying. |
| **Ant Challenge** | Teleports you to the center of the Ant Field. | Resets your position if you get cornered by ants. |
| **Snowbear Fight** | Only teleports bees if you have the "Frozen" debuff. | Bees continue to convert while you remain stuck. |
| **Lag Spikes** | May fail to teleport the player, only moving bees. | Useful for "unsticking" bee AI during server lag. |

### Trivia and Fun Facts

- **Inspiration**: The item is based on the samara fruit of maple trees. The spinning mechanic in-game mimics how these seeds travel long distances in the wind.
- **Cooldown History**: Prior to late 2022, Whirligigs had a 5-minute cooldown, making them much less viable for frequent use. This was removed to improve game flow.
- **The White Tunnel**: Aside from defeating Tunnel Bear or using the gray plate, the Whirligig is one of the only ways to exit the White Tunnel mid-run.

<Callout type="info" title="Ant Challenge Change">
In older versions of the game, using a Whirligig during the Ant Challenge would teleport you away and end the challenge. It has since been updated to move you to the center of the field instead, allowing you to continue the fight.
</Callout>
