<Section icon={Target} title="Core Gameplay and Wave Mechanics">

Once you enter the arena, the objective is simple: defeat as many ants as possible within a 5-minute time limit. The challenge ends if the timer hits zero, if you leave the field, or if your character dies. A unique mechanic of the **bee swarm simulator ant challenge** is the +100% Instant Conversion buff, which ensures you spend your time fighting rather than running back to your hive to empty your bag.

The difficulty scales through waves. To progress to the next wave, you must fill the honey meter on the side of the anthill by gathering pollen.

| Wave Feature | Description | Scaling Detail |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Pollen Requirement** | Starts at 4,000 pollen | Increases by 2,000+ per wave |
| **Ant Levels** | Starts at Level 1 and 2 | Levels increase every few waves |
| **Giant Ants** | Spawns every 5th wave | 2 Giants spawn at Wave 40 |
| **Lawnmowers** | Hazard appearing from Wave 8 | Deals 50 base damage |

<Callout type="warning" title="Hazard Alert: Lawnmowers">
Starting at Wave 8, white doors on the sides will flash red. This indicates a lawnmower is about to speed across the lane. These move faster and appear more frequently as you reach higher waves.
</Callout>

The variety of ants you face will change as the challenge progresses. During the first 15 rounds, you will mostly encounter Flying Ants. After Wave 15, Army Ants become significantly more common. Fire Ants are particularly dangerous as they leave trails of fire that deal damage over time.

</Section>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Ant Amulet Tiers and Rewards">

The primary reason players grind the **bee swarm simulator ant challenge** is for the Ant Amulet. This gear piece occupies a unique slot and provides various buffs to your hive, such as increased player move speed, white/red/blue pollen boosts, and critical power.

| Score Points | Amulet Tier | Reward Quality |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |

<Callout type="info" title="Reward Mechanics">
Each ant defeated grants 1 point. After the challenge ends, you can choose to keep your old amulet or replace it with the new one. Always compare the specific stat rolls (like Critical Chance or Bee Attack) rather than just looking at the tier.
</Callout>

Beyond amulets, the challenge provides a variety of items based on your score. These can include Sunflower Seeds, Strawberries, Blueberries, Pineapples, and even rare stickers like the **Left Facing Ant Sticker** or the **Ant Field Stamp Sticker**. In extremely rare cases, a x2 Convert Speed Voucher may drop, though this is considered nearly impossible by the community.
