The first three quests of the final block (Quests 16, 17, and 18) serve as an entry barrier to the high-tier rewards [1]. While they are not mechanically complex, they demand a staggering amount of Bloom collection and active gameplay participation [1]. 

To complete these quests without experiencing extreme burnout, players should rely on automation tools and coordinated team play [1]. For instance, collecting Blooms manually can take dozens of hours [1]. Utilizing a reliable macro program, such as the Revolution Macro, allows you to gather Blooms continuously while away from your computer [1].
