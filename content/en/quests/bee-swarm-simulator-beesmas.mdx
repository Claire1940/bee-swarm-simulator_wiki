<Section icon={Target} title="Snow Bear and Snowflake Farming">

Snowflakes are the primary currency for the Beesmas Catalog. While many players focus on defeating the Snow Bear as quickly as possible, this can actually be counterproductive. As the Snow Bear levels up, it becomes significantly harder to kill, eventually requiring a full team of end-game players.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Level Management">
    Keep your Snow Bear level only 1 or 2 levels higher than your hive level. This ensures you can solo most of the health bar.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The 75% Rule">
    Focus on dealing 75% damage (three-quarters) to the Snow Bear. Each quarter of health lost drops a significant amount of snowflakes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Avoid the Kill">
    If the timer is running out, do not panic if you don't kill it. The snowflakes dropped from the health milestones are often more valuable than the kill reward if it means keeping the level low.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="End-Event Push">
    Only in the final week of Beesmas should you call for help to push the Snow Bear to its maximum possible level for the final badge and rewards.
  </Step>
</Steps>

By following this method, you can farm snowflakes solo multiple times a day without the frustration of facing a Level 20+ Snow Bear that your hive cannot handle. This is the most efficient way to afford the expensive items in Bee Bear's Catalog, such as the Mythic Egg or the Festive Bean.
