Located strategically between the Red Cannon and the Special Sprout Summoner, he serves as an essential guide for new and veteran beekeepers alike. His questlines are designed to teach players the nuances of pollen collection and swarm management.

<Section icon={Target} title="Main Quest Progression">

Black Bear's quests start simple, focusing on basic fields like Sunflower and Dandelion, but they quickly scale in complexity. To succeed, you must understand the different types of pollen and how to maximize your gathering speed.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Early Game Basics">
    Focus on small fields like the Sunflower and Dandelion fields. These quests introduce you to the basic mechanics of pollen collection and reward small amounts of Honey.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Mid-Game Challenges">
    As you progress, you will need to start collecting specific colored pollen (Red, Blue, or White). This requires shifting your hive composition to include bees that specialize in these colors.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Late-Game Mastery">
    The final questlines involve massive amounts of pollen from high-tier fields like the Pepper Patch and Coconut Field. At this stage, utilizing items like Field Dice and various Extracts is mandatory.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Efficiency Boost">
Don't ignore the importance of badge progression. Black Bear often rewards items that help you earn badges faster, which in turn permanently boosts your honeymaking stats.
</Callout>
