<Section icon={Swords} title="Attack Patterns and Dodge Mechanics">

The Coconut Crab utilizes a highly predictable rotation consisting of two primary attack phases [secondary: web]. Understanding these patterns is essential for survival, as a single hit from its falling coconuts can instantly defeat under-geared players [secondary: web].

During the Claw Phase, the crab attempts to line itself up with your character and jabs rapidly with its claws [secondary: web]. It performs seven quick jabs, pauses to reposition, and repeats this sequence twice more for a total of 21 strikes [secondary: web]. Each strike deals 40 base damage before defense is applied [secondary: web]. You can easily dodge this phase by continuously moving or gliding from side to side across the field [secondary: web].

During the Coconut Phase, the crab retreats to the center of the back wall and jumps eight times [secondary: web]. Each jump launches a large coconut that targets the field [secondary: web]. The first coconut always targets your exact position, while the remaining seven land randomly [secondary: web]. These deal a devastating 150 base damage and can easily prove lethal [secondary: web].

| Attack Type | Damage | Mechanics | How to Dodge |
| Claw Attack | **40 Damage** | Jabs quickly at the player 21 times total | Walk or glide past the crab as it moves closer |
| Coconut Attack | **150 Damage** | Drops 8 large coconuts after jumping 8 times | Jump after the crab's second jump or use ledge glitch |
