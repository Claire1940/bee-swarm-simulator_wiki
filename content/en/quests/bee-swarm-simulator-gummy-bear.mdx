<Section icon={Shield} title="Lair Mechanics and Survival Strategies">

Entering the lair of the **bee swarm simulator gummy bear** is a high-stakes encounter. If you approach him without owning at least one piece of his specialized shop gear, he will initiate a dialogue that ends with your character's immediate demise. This is a unique mechanic where an NPC acts as an environmental hazard.

**The Death Dialogue Sequence:**
1. "What's sweeter than honey?"
2. "Glorious gumdrops... they're just the start!"
3. "A sweet and sour, ooey-gooey universe..."
4. "Can't you see it too?"
5. "What's that Gummy Bee? They're not seeing clearly?"
6. "YOU'RE ALWAYS RIGHT! GOO-dbye, beekeeper... HAH!"

After the final line, Gummy Bear uses the point emote and teleports you back to the spawn point, resetting your current session's pollen.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Preparation">
    Ensure you have at least 100 million Goo collected to earn the Hotshot Badge. If you intend to talk to him, save up for the Gummy Mask first.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Entrance">
    Go to the Ticket Tent in the 20 Bee Zone. Jump on top of the Gummy Bee statue to be teleported into the Gummy Lair.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Interaction">
    If you have the gear, walk up to him to claim Beesmas quests. If not, stay back or use a Whirligig immediately to escape.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Emergency Exit">
    If you accidentally trigger the dialogue, use a Micro-Converter to save your pollen or a Whirligig **before** the dialogue finishes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Survival Tool | Effective? | Limitation |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Whirligig** | Yes | Must be used before the dialogue ends |
| **Micro-Converter** | Yes | Only saves pollen; you still die |
| **Gummy Mask** | Yes | Permanent safety for dialogue |
| **Gummy Boots** | Yes | Permanent safety for dialogue |
| **Gummyballer** | Yes | Permanent safety for dialogue |

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Warning">
Even if you use a Whirligig to teleport back to your hive while the dialogue is on your screen, the script will continue. Once the last line is "spoken," you will still die at your hive unless the dialogue was closed or the teleport happened before the script's final execution.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Elite Gummy Gear and Shop Requirements">

To truly join the "Gummy Army" and interact with the **bee swarm simulator gummy bear** as an ally, you must craft items from his secret shop. These items are among the most powerful in the game, specifically designed to maximize Goo collection and white flower pollen.

| Item | Primary Benefit | Key Ingredients |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Gummy Mask** | Gummy Morph Ability | 250 Glue, 100 Enzymes, 100 Oil |
| **Gummy Boots** | Infinite Goo Trail | 500 Glue, 250 Glitter, 250 Blue/Red Extracts |
| **Gummyballer** | Endgame White Tool | 1,000 Glue, 500 Caustic Wax, 100 Super Smoothies |

**Why the Gummy Mask is Essential:**
The Gummy Mask is usually the first "Endgame Mask" players aim for. It provides the **Gummy Morph** passive, which occasionally turns you into a Gummy Bear yourself, granting massive Goo collection boosts and making all flowers in the vicinity count as Goo-covered.

<Callout type="tip" title="Resource Management">
Glue is the hardest resource to obtain for Gummy gear. In 2026, the best ways to farm Glue are through the Glue Dispenser (daily), crafting in the Blender, and defeating the Stump Snail or Coconut Crab.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Zap} title="Historical Context: The Gummy Invasion">

The **bee swarm simulator gummy bear** wasn't always a permanent resident. He first appeared during the **Gummy Invasion** event in May 2018. During this time, he was a traveling bear who tasked players with 15 initiation quests to join his "Gummy Empire."

**Legacy Rewards from the Invasion:**
- **Gummy Bee Egg**: The ultimate reward for completing all 15 quests.
- **Tapioca Code**: A secret code revealed during the "Peace Through Goo-nity" quest.
- **Gummy Cannon**: An environmental object Gummy Bear used to fire Gumdrops at fields.

| Quest Name | Key Objective | Reward |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Begin The Goo Invasion** | Collect 500 Goo | 1 Ticket, 1 Gumdrop |
| **Total Gummification** | Collect 60,000 Goo | 1 Ticket, 5 Gumdrops |
| **Candy Conquest** | Collect 750,000 Goo | Gummy Bee Egg |

<Callout type="info" title="Trivia: Floating Bears">
Gummy Bear is one of only two bears in the game that float rather than stand on the ground (the other being Shadow Bear). He uses the Levitation Animation Pack to maintain his ghostly, translucent appearance.
