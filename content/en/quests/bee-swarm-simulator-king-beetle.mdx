<Section icon={Map} title="Locating the King Beetle Lair">

Finding the **bee swarm simulator king beetle** is the first challenge for many early-game players. Unlike the Rhino Beetles or Ladybugs found in open fields, the King Beetle resides in a dedicated instance known as the King Beetle Lair. This area is tucked away, requiring players to pay close attention to the map boundaries.

To find the entrance, head to the **Blue Flower Field**. Look for a small, dark opening in the cliffside behind the field. You will see a gate that requires at least **10 bees** to pass. If you haven't reached this hive size yet, focus on the basic collect-and-convert loop until you can hatch enough eggs.

<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Strategy and Best Bee Lineups">

Defeating the **bee swarm simulator king beetle** requires a mix of player movement and high-impact bee abilities. The beetle primarily attacks by charging in a straight line toward the player. If you stand still, you will take significant damage and potentially be knocked out of the lair, failing the encounter.

The most effective strategy is the **Circular Strafe**. By running in a wide circle around the perimeter of the lair, you force the King Beetle to constantly turn and miss its charges. This gives your bees ample time to fly in, deliver their attacks, and drop ability tokens.
