<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Guide to the Ant Challenge">

After completing Panda Bear's initial quests, he grants you access to the Ant Challenge. This challenge is an arena-style mini-game where you must defeat waves of ants within a strict time limit. The higher your score, the better the Ant Amulet you receive at the end of the run.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Obtain Ant Passes">
    Collect Ant Passes from Panda Bear's daily repeatables, claim them from the pass dispenser, or purchase them using Tickets.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Prepare Your Hive">
    Before entering, ensure your bees are fully rested and you have combat boosts active, such as Red Extract, Blue Extract, or Stingers.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Dodge and Attack">
    Inside the arena, constantly move to avoid the fire trails left by Fire Ants and the giant lawnmowers that sweep across the field.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Claim Your Amulet">
    Once the timer expires or your character loses all health, talk to Panda Bear to claim your new Ant Amulet, replacing your old one if the stats are superior.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Optimizing your performance in the Ant Challenge requires a balance between collecting pollen to keep your bees active and focusing purely on defeating the incoming waves. Using a Vicious Bee is highly recommended here, as its spike ability can clear entire groups of ants instantly.

| Amulet Tier | Score Required | Key Stat Boosts |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Bronze | 1 - 49 | Convert Rate, Bee Attack |
| Silver | 50 - 99 | Critical Chance, Red/Blue Pollen |
| Gold | 100 - 149 | Player Movespeed, Capacity |
| Diamond | 150 - 249 | Critical Power, White Pollen |
| Supreme | 250+ | All Pollen, Ability Pollen |

<Callout type="tip" title="Stinger Management">
Only use Stingers during the Ant Challenge if you are close to reaching a new high score or transitioning to a higher amulet tier. Saving Stingers for the Vicious Bee capture or wild bosses is generally more efficient.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Panda Bear Quest Rewards and Milestones">

Completing quests for Panda Bear rewards you with valuable resources, including Honey, Royal Jellies, Tickets, and Ant Passes. The ultimate goal of the questline is to complete "Panda Bear's Final Quest," which rewards you with a highly coveted Star Treat. This item is used to turn any bee into its Gifted version, unlocking powerful hive bonuses.
