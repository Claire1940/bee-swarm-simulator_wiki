<Section icon={Target} title="Round-by-Round Progression Strategy">

Every run of the **bee swarm simulator robo bear challenge** follows a structured loop divided into distinct phases: selecting a quest, choosing a starting field, harvesting pollen while battling mechanical enemies, and spending your hard-earned cogs at the upgrade shop before the next round begins.

Managing your time efficiently during the early rounds sets the foundation for surviving the brutal scaling of later stages. If you finish a round quickly, the remaining time converts into bonus cogs, which are critical for purchasing premium mechanical upgrades.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Quest Selection and Evaluation">
    Evaluate the three randomized quests presented by Robo Bear. Look for fields where you possess permanent gear boosts or fields that align with your current hive color. Avoid quests requiring massive amounts of goo unless you have a Gummy Mask equipped.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Early Rush (Rounds 1-5)">
    Focus on absolute speed. Mobs in these rounds have low health. Clear them immediately using your collector and allow your bees to gather pollen instantly. Do not use heavy consumables like micro-converters yet; save them for later.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Mid-Run Scaling (Rounds 6-10)">
    Mechanical enemies like Cogmowers begin to spawn with shield mechanics. Keep your focus tokens at a stack of five at all times to maximize critical strike chances. Use your sprinklers immediately upon entering the designated field to sustain flower growth.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Late-Game Survival (Rounds 11+)">
    Quests now require tens of millions of pollen within narrow time windows. Rotate between active fields if your quest allows it, and deploy high-value items like field dice or tropical drinks to amplify your base gathering multipliers.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Cog Economy">
Do not hoard your cogs. Cogs do not carry over between challenge attempts. Spending them on immediate upgrades like bee attack power, player movement speed, or instant conversion percentages is always better than saving for an expensive upgrade you might not survive to buy.
</Callout>

To maximize your upgrade choices between rounds, consult this priority matrix to determine where to invest your accumulated cog currency.

<Section icon={Swords} title="Defeating Mechanical Enemies and Faction Waves">

Combat during the challenge differs significantly from standard bug hunts in the main world fields. Mechanical mobs scale in health, defense, and damage output with every passing round. Furthermore, certain rounds trigger specialized faction waves, spawning armies of theme-specific enemies that can completely disrupt your farming patterns.

Understanding enemy attack indicators and prioritizing targets ensures your character does not take lethal damage, which inflicts a severe time penalty on your active quest clock.

| Enemy Name | Primary Attack Behavior | Weakness / Counter Strategy |
|---|---|---|
| **Mechsquito** | Ranged flying attacks that drain player health | High focus stacks and precise frog slime hits |
| **Cogmower** | Rapid charging lines that destroy field flowers | Dodge perpendicularly and deploy high-level spikes |
| **Robo Ant** | Swarms the player in high numbers to block movement | Radial bomb tokens and high-level fire fields |
| **Mega Cogmower** | Massive health pool with area-of-effect stomps | Require continuous level 15+ bee attacks and stingers |

<Callout type="warning" title="Character Safety">
If your character health drops to zero during a challenge round, you do not instantly fail the run, but you suffer a massive 30-second deduction from your remaining round timer. Keep moving in circular patterns around the field edges to avoid getting cornered by charging Cogmowers.
</Callout>

When dealing with Golden or Glitched variants of these mechanical units, prioritize using combat-boosting consumables. Activating a single stinger at the start of a combat-heavy wave grants your entire hive a significant pierce bonus, allowing your bees to bypass mechanical armor plating efficiently.
