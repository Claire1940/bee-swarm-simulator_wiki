<Section icon={Trophy} title="Bee Swarm Simulator Science Bear Rewards: Enhancement & Translators">

Completing quests for Science Bear yields two incredibly powerful rewards: **Science Enhancement** stacks and **Translators**. 

Science Enhancement is a permanent passive buff that increases your bees' honey conversion rate. Each stack increases this rate by 25%. With 31 quests available in his primary series, you can reach a maximum of Level 31 Science Enhancement, which grants a massive 775% honey production rate bonus.

| Science Enhancement Level | Conversion Rate Bonus | Honey per Second (Basic Bee) |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Level 1 | 25% | 25 |
| Level 5 | 125% | 45 |
| Level 10 | 250% | 70 |
| Level 15 | 375% | 95 |
| Level 20 | 500% | 120 |
| Level 25 | 625% | 145 |
| Level 31 | 775% | 175 |

Beyond conversion rates, Science Bear is the sole source of Translators in the game. These devices allow you to communicate with non-bear NPCs on the mountain, unlocking unique repeatable quests and the highly profitable Stick Bug Challenge.

| Translator Number | Quest Milestone | Target NPCs |
| --- | --- | --- |
| First Translator | 21st Quest (Breaking Down Badges) | Gifted Bucko Bee / Gifted Riley Bee / Stick Bug |
| Second Translator | 26th Quest (Beesperanto) | Gifted Bucko Bee / Gifted Riley Bee / Stick Bug |
| Third Translator | 31st Quest (Epistemological Endeavor) | Gifted Bucko Bee / Gifted Riley Bee / Stick Bug |

<Callout type="tip" title="Translator Allocation Strategy">
It is highly recommended to give your first two Translators to Gifted Riley Bee and Gifted Bucko Bee. This unlocks their repeatable quests, allowing you to farm Red and Blue Extracts early. Save Stick Bug for your third and final Translator, as the Stick Bug Challenge is difficult to solo in the mid-game.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Quest Walkthrough">

Science Bear's questline is divided into two major phases: the Initial Series (16 quests) and the Translator Series (15 quests). The difficulty scales significantly as you progress, shifting from simple pollen collection to complex token farming and crafting.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Early-Game Foundations (Quests 1-10)">
    Focus on unlocking new bee types and gathering basic colored pollen. Use Royal Jellies to transform duplicate basic bees into rare or epic types to meet the "Discover Bee Types" requirements easily. Keep an eye out for Ladybugs and Rhino Beetles to complete the mob defeat requirements.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Mid-Game Badge Grinds (Quests 11-20)">
    During this phase, you must earn specific badges (such as the Sunflower or Bamboo badges) and start collecting Ability Tokens. Keep Haste and Focus bees in your hive to generate the required tokens quickly. You will also need to defeat harder mobs like Spiders and Werewolves.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Translator Era (Quests 21-31)">
    These quests require extensive crafting in the Blender (located in the Badge Bearer's Guild) and massive amounts of Goo collection. You must use Gumdrops on boosted fields to gather millions of Goo. The final quest, completed in 2026 meta runs, requires collecting 10,000 of several token types.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Quest Name | Notable Requirements | Key Rewards |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Preliminary Research (1st) | Discover 5 Bee Types, 20k White Pollen | 31,415 Honey, 1x Science Enhancement |
| Biology Study (2nd) | Discover 6 Bee Types, Defeat 8 Ladybugs | 47,806 Honey, 1x Science Enhancement |
| Breaking Down Badges (21st) | Earn 1 Sunflower & 2 Battle Badges, 524 Blue Boost Tokens | 700,127 Honey, 1x Science Enhancement, 1x Translator |
| Beesperanto (30th) | Obtain Carpenter Bee, Craft 64 Ingredients, 1,280 Focus Tokens | 1x Science Enhancement, 1x Translator |
| Epistemological Endeavor (31st) | 1B Sunflower Pollen, 10k Honey/Boost/Focus Tokens, 1k Rage Tokens | 340M Honey, 1x Science Enhancement, 1x Translator |

</Callout>

Completing these drive quests requires advanced gear and preparation. The Spark Staff, while generally considered an inefficient tool for general farming, is mandatory for completing specific portions of these quests.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Top Tips for Fast Science Bear Progression">

To complete Science Bear's research efficiently and claim your Translators without endless grinding, employ these professional strategies:

- **Utilize Field Boosters**: Always check the Red, Blue, and Mountain Top Field Boosters before starting a grind. Matching a 2x or 3x field boost with your quest requirements will cut your farming time in half.
- **Blender Management**: Science Bear's later quests require crafting dozens of items. Always keep a steady supply of Blueberries, Strawberries, Pineapples, and Royal Jellies to craft Gumdrops, Enzymes, and Oils in bulk.
- **Sprout Sharing**: Cooperate with other players to pop Magic Sprouts. The Sprout Tokens collected count toward your quest totals, and sharing the work makes growing rare sprouts much faster.
