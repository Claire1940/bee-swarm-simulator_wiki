<Section icon={Map} title="Unlocking the Stick Bug Challenge">

The **bee swarm simulator stick bug** is a unique boss NPC located behind the 10-Bee Gate, standing right next to Science Bear. Unlike standard field bosses, this encounter is an organized event known as the Stick Bug Challenge. Participating in this event is the primary method for players to obtain the highly coveted Stick Bug Amulets, which provide substantial permanent buffs to your hive.

Before you can interact with the boss, you must secure a way to communicate with it. This requires obtaining a Translator, a rare quest item provided by Science Bear during his extensive questline. Because Translators are limited, deciding when and how to spend them is a critical progression choice.

| Requirement | Detail |
| :--- | :--- |
| Location | Behind the 10-Bee Gate, near Science Bear |
| Unlock Item | 1 Translator (given to Stick Bug NPC) |
| Cooldown | 36 hours for free summon |
| Cost | 50 Tickets for instant summon |
| Level | Recommended hive level 10 or higher |

Once unlocked, you can trigger the challenge for free once every 36 hours. If you wish to run the challenge during the cooldown period, you must spend 50 Tickets. Alternatively, another player who has unlocked the challenge can summon it for you, allowing you to participate and earn rewards without spending your own resources.

<Callout type="warning" title="Translator Allocation">
Do not rush to use your first Translator on the Stick Bug unless you have a strong attack hive. Many players prefer giving their first Translator to Gifted Riley Bee or Gifted Bucko Bee to unlock specialized quests, saving the Stick Bug for later when their bees are higher level.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Mechanics and Field Rotation">

When the challenge begins, the **bee swarm simulator stick bug** will jump into a random field and shield itself. It will spawn Stick Minions and totems across various fields. While these totems are active, the boss gains a massive defense modifier, making it nearly immune to damage.

Understanding how to manage these totems and coordinate your movements is the key to surviving the higher levels of the encounter.

The boss levels up every time you deplete its health bar. With each level, its max health increases, and it spawns more aggressive minions. The following tables outline the scaling mechanics and field-specific strategies.

| Level Range | HP per Level | Minion Count | Defense Modifier |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Levels 1-5 | 5,000 - 25,000 | 2 - 4 | Baseline defense |
| Levels 6-10 | 35,000 - 95,000 | 5 - 8 | 15% damage reduction |
| Levels 11-15 | 120,000 - 300,000 | 9 - 12 | 30% damage reduction |
| Levels 16+ | 400,000+ | 12+ | 50% damage reduction |

Depending on where the boss lands, your movement and positioning must adapt. Different fields offer unique challenges due to their size and layout.

| Field | Obstacles | Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Bamboo Field | High blue flower density | Use blue bomb tokens to clear surrounding area |
| Mushroom Field | Small area, tight spaces | Watch out for close-range minion spawns |
| Clover Field | Even layout | Good for general gathering and spike placement |
| Cactus Field | Spiky terrain | Keep moving to avoid getting trapped by minions |

<Callout type="info" title="Minion Shield Mechanic">
The defense modifier is directly proportional to the number of active Stick Minions. If you ignore the minions to focus solely on the boss, your damage output will drop significantly. Always clear the minions first.
</Callout>

Your primary goal in completing the **bee swarm simulator stick bug** challenge is to earn a high-tier Stick Bug Amulet. These amulets replace your current amulet slot and offer powerful passive stats that scale with the quality of the amulet.

Amulet quality is determined by the score you achieve during the challenge. The higher the level of the boss you defeat, the higher your score multiplier.

| Amulet Tier | Score Required | Key Guaranteed Stats |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Bronze | 1,000,000 | Capacity, Bee Attack |
| Silver | 5,000,000 | Higher Capacity, Bee Attack, 1 random boost |
| Gold | 25,000,000 | Substantial Capacity, +2 random boosts |
| Diamond | 100,000,000 | High Capacity, +3 random boosts, color bonus |
| Supreme | 500,000,000 | Maximum Capacity, +4 random boosts, critical power |
