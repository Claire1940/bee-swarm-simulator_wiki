<Section icon={Map} title="Locating the Bee Swarm Simulator Tunnel Bear">

The **bee swarm simulator tunnel bear** serves as one of the most significant skill checks for players transitioning from the early game into the mid-game progression phase. Unlike field-based enemies, this boss resides in a specialized instance known simply as "The Tunnel." Accessing this area requires players to have unlocked the 10-bee gate, located near the Instant Converter and the Pro Shop.

Once you pass through the 10-bee gate, you will find a dark opening in the wall. Walking into this dark corridor triggers the boss encounter. It is important to note that the Tunnel Bear does not spawn if the 48-hour cooldown is still active from your previous victory. You can check your "Defeated" status in the badges or quest menu, though most players simply keep a mental timer or check the tunnel entrance for the bear's physical presence.

<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Strategy and Mechanics">

The fight against the **bee swarm simulator tunnel bear** is unique because of the restricted movement. The bear moves slowly toward you from the far end of the tunnel. If it touches you, you take massive damage, usually resulting in an instant reset. Your goal is to lead the bear back and forth through the tunnel while your bees attack it.

Because the tunnel is narrow, the bear's hitbox takes up almost the entire width. However, there is just enough space to maneuver around it if you use the wall-hugging technique. Most players prefer to walk to the very end of the tunnel, wait for the bear to get close, and then carefully slip past it to run to the other end.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Trigger the Spawn">
    Walk into the tunnel until the boss music starts and the bear emerges from the darkness.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Backpedal Slowly">
    Walk backward toward the entrance while your bees engage. Stay just out of the bear's reach to maximize bee attack time.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Turnaround">
    When you reach the entrance, hug the left or right wall and sprint past the bear as it reaches the threshold.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Repeat and Burst">
    Continue this back-and-forth cycle. Use your **Vicious Bee's Impale** ability as soon as it off cooldown to deal massive burst damage.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Movement Mastery">
Using **Hasty Bees** or speed-boosting gear like the **Beelzebub Boots** makes the turnaround phase much safer. The faster you move, the less time you spend in the "danger zone" next to the bear's hitbox.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Loot Table and Reward Tiers">

Defeating the **bee swarm simulator tunnel bear** is highly rewarding, especially for players looking to craft mid-to-late game gear. The rewards are split into "Guaranteed Drops" and "Rare RNG Drops." Your first-ever kill of the Tunnel Bear grants a massive one-time bonus, including a Silver Egg and several Star Jellies.

Subsequent kills provide a variety of treats, materials, and a high chance for localized field boosts. Many players farm the Tunnel Bear specifically for **Star Jellies**, which are required to transform bees into their "Gifted" versions, unlocking powerful hive bonuses.
