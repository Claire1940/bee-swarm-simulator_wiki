<Section icon={Map} title="Understanding Bee Swarm Simulator Bond Mechanics">

In the world of Bee Swarm Simulator, your hive's strength is not just determined by the rarity of your bees, but by their level. **Bee swarm simulator bond** represents the total experience points a bee has accumulated. As a bee gains Bond, it levels up, which directly scales its performance in every field. If you ignore the Bond system, your bees will struggle to hit high-level monsters, and your pollen collection will remain stagnant even with expensive gear.

Leveling up bees is a linear progression of power. For every level gained, a bee increases its energy, attack damage, and pollen collection stats. Furthermore, certain gates and challenges, such as the 35-bee zone or specific NPC trials, become significantly easier when your hive average level is 10 or higher.

| Bee Level | Approximate Bond Required | Primary Stat Benefits |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Level 1-5 | 100 - 1,500 | Basic energy and attack scaling |
| Level 6-8 | 4,000 - 15,000 | Improved accuracy against field bugs |
| Level 9-11 | 40,000 - 250,000 | Mandatory for Coconut Crab combat |
| Level 12-15 | 1M - 15M | Endgame farming efficiency |
| Level 16+ | 40M+ | Specialized hive optimization |

<Callout type="info" title="Why Leveling Matters">
Bees have a "Miss" chance when attacking enemies of a higher level. If your bee is level 8 and the monster is level 10, your bees will frequently miss their attacks, making boss fights nearly impossible. Increasing your **bee swarm simulator bond** is the only way to fix this.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="The Best Treats and Fruits for Leveling">

Feeding your bees is the fastest way to increase their **bee swarm simulator bond**. While bees gain a small amount of Bond naturally while gathering pollen (1 Bond per pollen collection cycle), this is far too slow for significant progress. Instead, you must utilize Treats, Fruits, and specialized items.

The game features several types of food, each with a base Bond value. However, the real secret to efficiency lies in "liked" foods. For example, feeding a Strawberry to a Red Bee provides significantly more Bond than feeding it to a Blue Bee.

<Section icon={Target} title="Mother Bear Leveling Roadmap">

Mother Bear is the primary NPC responsible for teaching players about **bee swarm simulator bond**. Her questline is designed to guide you through the process of hatching, feeding, and leveling your first 25 bees. Completing her tasks is the most efficient way to earn massive amounts of Treats without spending your hard-earned honey.

Follow these steps to maximize your progress through Mother Bear's training:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Feeding">
    Start by feeding your bees the basic Treats earned from Black Bear's early quests. Focus on getting your entire hive to level 4.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Type-Specific Quests">

<Section icon={Shield} title="Maximizing Bond Multipliers and Puppy Bee">

As you move into the mid-to-late game, the amount of Bond required for a single level becomes astronomical. To keep progressing, you must utilize Bond multipliers. The most significant multiplier in Bee Swarm Simulator comes from the **Puppy Bee**.

While Puppy Bee is often considered "weak" in terms of pollen collection, its hive bonus is arguably the most powerful for long-term account growth. When Puppy Bee is Gifted, it provides a **20% Bond from Treats** bonus. Combined with the Moon Amulet and specific Beequips, you can reduce the total honey cost of leveling your hive by millions.

| Multiplier Source | Bonus Type | Requirement |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Puppy Bee (Normal)** | +10% Bond from Treats | Own Puppy Bee |
| **Gifted Puppy Bee** | +20% Bond from Treats | Use Star Treat on Puppy Bee |
| **Moon Amulet** | Up to +15% Bond from Treats | Roll Moon Charms at Moon Statue |
| **Beequips** | Variable % | Equip specific items to bees |

<Callout type="warning" title="Leveling Trap">
Do not spend billions of honey on treats until you have a Gifted Puppy Bee and a high-roll Moon Amulet. Without these multipliers, you are effectively throwing away 20-30% of your honey.
</Callout>
