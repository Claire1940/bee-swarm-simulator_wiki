<Section icon={Map} title="Daily Glue Farming Routes & Methods">

To build a consistent stockpile of glue, you must integrate daily dispenser claims and specific field runs into your gameplay routine. The most reliable passive source of glue is the Glue Dispenser located inside the Gummy Bear's Lair. To access this secret area, you must stand on the gumdrop icon next to the 20-Bee Gate and use a Gumdrop, which temporarily transports you to the lair.

Once inside, you can claim free glue from the large dispenser once every 22 hours. The exact number of glues you receive scales directly with your Goo Badge tier, making active goo farming highly beneficial for your daily passive yield.

<Section icon={Target} title="Crafting Bee Swarm Simulator Glue">

Active crafting in the Blender is the fastest way to acquire glue if you have already claimed your daily dispenser reward. The Blender is located inside the 15-Bee Zone next to the Red Headquarters. The recipe for a single glue requires 50 Gumdrops and 1 Royal Jelly.

Because crafting 250 glues for the Gummy Mask requires a staggering 12,500 Gumdrops, you must optimize your ingredient gathering. You can craft Gumdrops in the Blender using common field fruits (Strawberries, Blueberries, and Pineapples) or redeem active promo codes to instantly boost your ingredient inventory.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Collect Raw Fruits">
    Farm Pineapples in the Pineapple Patch, Strawberries in the Strawberry Field, and Blueberries in the Bamboo Forest.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Craft Gumdrops in the Blender">
    Convert 3 of each matching fruit into 1 Gumdrop. Use the Blender's bulk crafting option to save active playtime.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Redeem Active Promo Codes">
    Input active codes like `fourtunate` (4 gumdrops), `15MMembers` (15 gumdrops), and `BeesBuzz123` (10 gumdrops) to quickly pad your inventory.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute the Glue Recipe">
    Combine 50 Gumdrops and 1 Royal Jelly in the Blender. Each crafting cycle takes 30 seconds to complete.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| 1 Glue | 50 | 1 | 30 Seconds |

<Section icon={Swords} title="Mob Drops & Event Sources">

Defeating aggressive mobs is an excellent passive supplement to your daily crafting routine. While mob drop rates are subject to chance, maintaining a strict extermination schedule ensures a steady trickle of extra glue and crafting ingredients over time.

The Stump Snail and Coconut Crab offer the highest yield of glue upon defeat, though they require high-level bees and significant time investments. For lower-level players, the Werewolf in the Clover Field and the Spider in the Spider Field are the most accessible mobs with a realistic chance to drop glue.

| Mob | Drop Chance | Average Glue | Combat Advice |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Spider | Low Chance | 1 Glue | Defeat quickly using Rage or Vicious Bees |
| Werewolf | Medium Chance | 1-2 Glue | Lead into Clover corner for easy damage |
| Coconut Crab | High Chance | 1-5 Glue | Dodge falling coconuts, use level 10+ bees |
| Stump Snail | Guaranteed | 5-15 Glue | Leave hive farming overnight on Stump Field |

<Callout type="warning" title="Stump Snail Grind">
The Stump Snail has 30 million HP. Do not attempt to actively fight it unless your hive is at least level 8 to 10. Instead, let your bees attack it passively while you are away from your keyboard to avoid wasting active playtime.
</Callout>
