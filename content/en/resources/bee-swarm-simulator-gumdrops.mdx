<Section icon={Construction} title="How to Farm Gumdrops Fast">

To reach the end-game gear, you will need thousands of **bee swarm simulator gumdrops**. Relying solely on codes is impossible for long-term growth. You must establish a farming routine that utilizes every mechanic available in the mountain.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Utilize the Gumdrop Dispenser">
    Located in the 20-Bee Zone, this dispenser provides free gumdrops every 22 hours. If you are a member of the Bee Swarm Simulator Club, you receive even more. This is the most consistent "passive" way to build your stash.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Master Mob Farming">
    Certain mobs have a higher probability of dropping gumdrops. Focus on defeating the **Werewolf** (15-Bee Zone) and the **Spider** (5-Bee Zone) on cooldown. While the drops are small (usually 1-3), they add up significantly over a week of active play.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Blender Crafting Method">
    Once you unlock the 15-Bee Zone, the Blender becomes your best friend. You can craft 1 Gumdrop using 3 Strawberries, 3 Blueberries, and 3 Pineapples. While this consumes your fruit, it is the only way to "force" a large amount of gumdrops in a short period.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Complete Brown Bear Quests">
    Brown Bear offers repeatable quests that frequently reward gumdrops and tickets. Tickets can also be used at the Gumdrop Dispenser to purchase gumdrops (1 ticket = 3 gumdrops), though this is generally considered expensive for early-game players.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Dispenser Efficiency">
If you have the "Wealth Clock" buff active, try to time your dispenser visits together. While the clock doesn't directly increase gumdrop yield, staying in-game for the dispenser cooldowns ensures you are maximizing your ticket income, which can be converted back into gumdrops if needed.
</Callout>
