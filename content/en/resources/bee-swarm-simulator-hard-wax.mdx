<Section icon={Map} title="Bee Swarm Simulator Hard Wax: Mechanics and Upgrades">

Hard Wax is a specialized inventory item added during the late 2021 expansion. It serves a dual purpose: acting as a crucial ingredient in high-tier crafting recipes and as a consumable upgrade material for your Beequips. When applied to a Beequip, it has a set probability of enhancing the item's statistics, making it highly valuable for players looking to optimize their hive's performance.

Applying Hard Wax to a Beequip carries a risk. Each attempt has a 60% chance of success, which triggers a notification confirming the improvement. If the upgrade fails, the wax is consumed with no changes made to the Beequip. This makes mass accumulation necessary for players seeking perfect stat rolls.

| Upgrade Metric | Statistical Value | Operational Detail |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Success Chance | 60% | Successfully improves Beequip stats |
| Failure Chance | 40% | Consumes wax with no stat changes |
| Upgrade Value | 2 | Value added to the Beequip profile |
| Perfect 5x Success | 7.776% | Probability of 5 consecutive upgrades |

<Callout type="warning" title="Upgrade Risk Warning">
Hard Wax is the only wax in the game that can fail to make any changes to a Beequip while still consuming the item. Because the probability of achieving a perfect five-stage upgrade is approximately 1 in 13 Beequips, you should avoid wasting this resource on low-tier or temporary equipment.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Crafting Recipes and Blender Guide">

While drops and quests provide excellent boosts, crafting is the only way to guarantee a steady supply of Hard Wax. The Blender, located near the 15-Bee Gate, allows you to combine lower-tier materials to create Hard Wax.

To craft a single Hard Wax, you must gather Soft Waxes, Enzymes, Bitter Berries, and Royal Jellies. This recipe requires a balanced mix of field-harvested resources and crafted sub-materials.

| Required Ingredient | Quantity Needed | Primary Acquisition Method |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Soft Wax | 3 | Craft in Blender or harvest Pesticide Planters |
| Enzymes | 3 | Craft with Pineapples or secure mob drops |
| Bitter Berries | 33 | Defeat bug mobs or clear active Puffshrooms |
| Royal Jellies | 33 | Purchase from shops or craft in Blender |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Secure Soft Waxes">
    Craft Soft Waxes in the Blender using 5 Honeysuckles, 5 Royal Jellies, and 10 Sunflower Seeds, or collect them passively from Pesticide and Clay Planters.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Gather Enzymes and Royal Jellies">
    Farm the Pineapple Patch to gather the raw materials needed to craft Enzymes, and accumulate Royal Jellies through active field farming or direct purchase.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Harvest Bitter Berries">
    Participate in Puffshroom clearing events. Rare and Epic Puffshrooms drop high quantities of Bitter Berries, which are vital for completing the craft.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute Craft at the Blender">
    Navigate to the Blender near the 15-Bee Gate, input the collected ingredients, and initiate the crafting process to receive your Hard Wax.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Crafting Efficiency Success">
By keeping a steady stock of raw ingredients like Pineapples and Sunflower Seeds, you can continuously run Blender crafts. This systematic approach completely bypasses the random nature of planter drops, ensuring you always have enough wax for your next upgrade.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Hard Wax Crafting Applications">

Hard Wax is not just an endgame upgrade consumable; it is also a fundamental crafting component for some of the most powerful items in the game. Saving your wax for these recipes is critical for long-term progression.

To unlock every single-purchase item that requires Hard Wax, you must accumulate a total of 465 Hard Waxes. This includes crafting high-tier utility items and advanced waxes like Swirled Wax.

| Crafted Output | Hard Wax Cost | Additional Ingredients Required |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Swirled Wax | 3 | 9 Soft Waxes, 6 Purple Potions, 3333 Royal Jellies |
| Loaded Dice | 3 | 3 Smooth Dice, 3 Oils, 1 Glue |

<Callout type="info" title="Late-Game Crafting Priority">
Swirled Wax and Loaded Dice are instrumental for late-game boosting and gear progression. Do not use all your Hard Waxes on Beequip upgrades until you have secured enough raw materials to craft these essential progression items.
</Callout>
