<Section icon={Swords} title="Renewable Farming: Mobs, Quests, and Sprouts">

If you are looking for active, renewable ways to secure extracts without waiting for planters to grow, you must focus on mob runs, Riley Bee quests, and Sprout farming. These methods can be repeated daily to maintain a steady influx of crafting ingredients.

Riley Bee, located in the Red HQ, offers repeatable quests that are incredibly lucrative. Every single quest you complete for Riley Bee is guaranteed to reward you with exactly one Red Extract, regardless of whether it is your first quest or your five-hundredth.

Additionally, certain mobs have a designated chance to drop extracts when defeated.

| Mob Name | Primary Location | Drop Chance | Spawn Cooldown |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Ladybug** | Clover, Strawberry, Mushroom | Low Chance | 5 Minutes |
| **Scorpion** | Rose Field, Cactus Field | Medium Chance | 20 Minutes |
| **King Beetle** | King Beetle Lair | 50% Chance | 30 Hours |

Defeating Ladybugs and Scorpions on cooldown is highly recommended. If you use a macro, ensure your settings are configured to automatically path through the Strawberry and Rose fields to clear these mobs. King Beetle is also highly recommended, as it features a 50% chance to drop either a Red Extract or a Blue Extract every 30 hours.

<Callout type="tip" title="Sprout Farming in Red Fields">
