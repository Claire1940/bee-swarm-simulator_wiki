<Section icon={Map} title="How to Obtain Bee Swarm Simulator Soft Wax">

Acquiring a healthy stockpile of Soft Wax requires a hybrid approach of active farming and passive crafting. Relying on a single source will slow down your progress, so integrating multiple methods into your daily routine is the most efficient path.

The most reliable active method is utilizing the Blender in the 15-Bee Zone. However, raw farming through planters, Puffshrooms, and mob drops will save you millions of Honey and valuable crafting materials over time.

### The Blender Crafting Workflow

If you have a surplus of basic ingredients, the Blender is your fastest route to bulk wax. Follow these steps to maximize your crafting efficiency:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Gather Raw Materials">
    Collect Sunflower Seeds from the Sunflower Field, harvest Honeysuckles from active planters, and secure Royal Jellies from star passes or mob drops.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Access the 15-Bee Zone Blender">
    Travel to the Blender located near the Blue HQ. Ensure you have enough Honey to cover the crafting fees.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Queue Batch Crafting">
    Craft Soft Wax in batches. Avoid using all your Sunflower Seeds at once, as they are also needed for feeding gifted bees.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Passive Farming and Field Selection

Planters are an exceptional passive source of Soft Wax. Placing the right planter in its preferred field guarantees periodic wax drops upon harvesting. Additionally, participating in active map events like Puffshroom spawns and firefly cycles will supplement your inventory.

| Farming Method | Recommended Location | Drop Reliability | Key Requirements |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Paper Planter** | Sunflower Field, Clover Field | High | 1-hour growth time |
| **Plastic Planter** | Dandelion Field, Mushroom Field | Moderate | 2-hour growth time |
| **Puffshroom Pops** | Any active field | Moderate | High combat/pollen power |
| **Firefly Cycles** | Nighttime fields | Low | Active collection at night |
| **Mob Defeat** | Werewolf (15-Bee), Spider (10-Bee) | Low | Standard mob cooldowns |
