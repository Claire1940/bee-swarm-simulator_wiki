<Section icon={Map} title="Optimal Daily Ticket Farming Routes">

To maintain a steady stream of tickets without relying solely on quests or codes, you must establish an efficient daily farming routine. This route combines time-gated mechanics, mob combat, and map exploration.

By structuring your play sessions around these activities, you can easily generate 50 to 100 tickets per day through active gameplay.

<Callout type="info" title="The Wealth Clock Mechanic">
The Wealth Clock is located near the 5-Bee Gate. You can claim it once every hour. Each consecutive claim increases the ticket reward by 1, capping at **5 tickets per claim**. Maintaining this streak is the most consistent passive ticket source in the game.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Claim the Wealth Clock">
    Begin your session by running to the 5-Bee Gate and claiming your Wealth Clock ticket. Set a timer to return to this spot every hour to keep your ticket multiplier at the maximum of 5 tickets.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Execute the Low-Level Mob Run">
    Clear the starter fields. Defeat Beetle in the Clover Field, Ladybugs in the Mushroom and Strawberry Fields, and Spider in the Spider Field. These mobs have short cooldowns and a high chance of dropping single tickets.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Defeat Elite Bosses">
    Move up the mountain to challenge the Werewolf in the Cactus Field, the King Beetle in his lair, and the Tunnel Bear. These bosses guarantee multiple tickets upon defeat, alongside rare crafting materials.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Utilize Ticket Dispensers Wisely">
    If you have excess honey, visit the Ticket Dispenser in the Mountain Top Shop. Be cautious: the honey cost per ticket scales up with every purchase, making it highly inefficient for early-game players.
  </Step>
</Steps>
